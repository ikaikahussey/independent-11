This Saturday, January 8, Masayo Loyd will offer a free introductory belly dancing class at the Waikiki Community Center, located at 310 Paoakalani Avenue. The class runs from 11:30 a.m. to 1:00 p.m. 

Lloyd teaches contemporary and modern Egyptian belly dancing techniques with Zill (finger cymbals) and veil. Participants will learn isolation and separation of various movements and combinations, as well as layering movements while playing finger cymbals. 

For further information and to register call (808) 923-1802 or visit www.waikikicommunitycenter.org.

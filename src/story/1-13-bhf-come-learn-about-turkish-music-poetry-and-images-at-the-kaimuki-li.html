KAIMUKI—Turkish Mystic Sufi musician Latif Bolat will present music, poetry, Sufi mystic stories, and images at the Kaimuki Public Library on Thursday, January 13 at 3:00 p.m. This free program will be held in the Young Adult Section.

Kaimuki Public Library is located at 1041 Koko Head Avenue. For more information, call the Library at (808) 733-8422 during normal hours of operation.  The program is subject to change.

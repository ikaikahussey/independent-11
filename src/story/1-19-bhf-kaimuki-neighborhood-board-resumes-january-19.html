The Kaimuki Neighborhood Board will meet Wednesday, January 19 from 7:15 p.m. to 9:00 p.m. in the Queen Liliuokalani Elementary School Cafeteria, located at 3633 Waialae Avenue.

New business to be discussed includes an update on the construction of left turn lanes on Harding Avenue and 11th and 5th Avenues by Wayne Yoshioka, Department of Transportation Services director, and Adrian Chang. There will also discuss a request for a traffic light at Harding and 12th Avenues.

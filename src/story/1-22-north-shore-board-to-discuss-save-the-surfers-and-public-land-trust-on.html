The North Shore Neighborhood Board will be holding its regular monthly meeting at the John Kalili Surf Center at Haleiwa Beach Park on Tuesday, January 25 at 7:00 p.m.

On the agenda are the City and County of Honolulu’s monthly reports from the Honolulu Police Department, Honolulu Fire Department, Mayor Peter Carlisle’s representative, Board of Water Supply, and Councilmember Ernie Martin.

Board business includes discussion on the Capital Improvement Project (CIP) Proposals to the City, the Public Land Trust and Save The Surfers.

The Board’s next meeting will take place on February 22 at 7:00 p.m. at the same location.

The complete January 25 meeting agenda can be found below.

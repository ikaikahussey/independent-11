The Hawaii Kai Neighborhood Board will meet Tuesday, January 25, at 7:00 p.m. in the Hahaione Elementary School Cafeteria, located at 595 Pepeekeo Street. Topics to be discussed include Honolulu City Council Bill 60 (2010), relating to fees and charges for use of parks and recreation facilities.

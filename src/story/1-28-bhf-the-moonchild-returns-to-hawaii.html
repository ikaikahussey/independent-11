Stoddard’s Hale presents blues veteran Travis “The Moonchild” Haddix (with Bobby Ingano, Pat Rawson, Dayton Arima and James Ganeko) at Anna’s (formerly known as Anna Bannana’s) on Friday January 28 at 7:00 p.m..  Ronstadt and Taylor will open. The cost is $20 at the door, and Memphis style BBQ will be available for purchase, thanks to Hog Island Smokehouse.

Anna’s is located at 2440 S Beretania St; (808) 946-5190.

Starting next week on Monday, October 4, Olelo Waianae Community Media Center (CMC) will be open to the public five days a week. The CMC will now be open an extra day on Saturdays from 9:00 a.m. to 2:00 p.m. and be closed on Fridays and Sundays. The extension to five days is in response to the community’s interest in being able to utilize CMC services and resources on the weekend.

Facilities are located at Waianae High School, 85-251 Farrington Highway.

The new hours of operations are:

Sundays: Closed
Mondays: 3:00 p.m. to 8:00 p.m.
Tuesdays: 3:00 p.m. to 8:00 p.m.
Wednesdays: 3:00 p.m. to 8:00 p.m.
Thursdays: 3:00 p.m. to 8:00 p.m.
Fridays: Closed
Saturdays: 9:00 a.m. to 2:00 p.m.

For more information, visit http://www.olelo.org/locations_waianae.htm.

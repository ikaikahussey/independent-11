Each week KSSK radio teams up with Rainbowtique to hand out the coach of the week award to a public nominated athletic figure among the OIA high school community. For the week of September 20 to 25, Coach Colin Lee was the given the honors. Coach Lee is Pearl City High School’s athletic health care trainer.

Lee started at Pearl City in 1997 when the field of athletic training was just starting to make its presence in Hawaii public high schools. Lee, alongside fellow athletic trainer Kim Imamoto, has helped rank Pearl City High School as one of the top athletic programs in Hawaii.

To nominate someone for the weekly recognition, visit http://www.ksskradio.com.

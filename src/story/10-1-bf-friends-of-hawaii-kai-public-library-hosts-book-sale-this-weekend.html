The Friends of Hawaii Kai Public Library will conduct a Book Sale at Hawaii Kai Public Library from Saturday, October 2 to Sunday, October 3 in the Meeting Room. The book sale hours will be from 10:00 a.m. to 3:00 p.m. on both days.

A large variety of gently-used audiobooks, books, CDs, DVDs, songbooks, and videos will be on sale. Come early for the best selection.

A special bag sale will be offered on Sunday, October 3 from 2:00 p.m. to 3:00 p.m. to help clear out the remaining inventory—customers may fill up a bag with items for just $3.00.

Proceeds from the book sale will help the Library fund programs and purchase informational materials, equipment, and supplies.

The Hawaii Kai Public Library is located at 249 Lunalilo Home Road. For more information, call the library at (808) 397-5833.

This Sunday, October 17, the Kapolei City Lions Club will host their second annual fundraiser benefit breakfast. The money made from the breakfast will go toward supporting the Lions Club. On the menu for the Sunday morning meal is scrambled eggs, portuguese sausage, rice, muffins, and assorted fruit. Tickets cost $6.00. Breakfast will start at 7:00 a.m. and last till 11:00 a.m. For those who want to help the lions club but don’t have the time, take-out service is available.

For the last seven years, the Kapolei City Lions Club has relied on the generosity of their community through donations towards support of their service projects, such as vision screenings in Leeward Oahu schools, and the semi-annual Blood Donation Drive. This year, they are looking to expand the range of public services they are involved with to include scholarships for Kapolei High School students.

For tickets, call Paul Berardo at (808) 342-2045, Marcie Cabico at (808) 693-8738, or Ampy Cabico at (808) 391-8989. Walk-in purchase is also accepted.

Kapolei City Lions Club breakfast
Sunday, October 17 from 7:00 a.m. to 11:00 a.m.

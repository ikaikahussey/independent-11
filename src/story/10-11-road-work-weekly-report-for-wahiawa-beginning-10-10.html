According to the City and County of Honolulu, there is no scheduled road work for the North Shore or Mililani for the week beginning October 10.

Wahiawa road work for the Department of Design Construction, Civil Division (Construction Management Branch), and Wastewater Division (Construction Management Branch) is listed below. 

Wahiawa is scheduled for road work at curb ramps at various locations including the Center Street mid block between North Cane Street and Lehua Street. Construction is for new curb ramps, sidewalks, and A.C. transition as well as the installation of pavement striping, markings, and markers. Work is expected to be completed this month.

Road work is normally done between the hours of 8:30 a.m. and 3:30 p.m. unless otherwise noted.

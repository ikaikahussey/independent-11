The Palolo Neighborhood Board will meet Wednesday, October 13 at 7:00 p.m. in the Palolo Elementary School Cafeteria, located at 2106 10th Avenue.

Featured on the agenda is a discussion of Hawaii’s Children First and the constitutional amendment allowing the governor to appoint the Board of Education. There have also been problems regarding bulky trash pickup in the East Oahu area, so the board is reminding the Palolo community to take out their bulky items by the morning of October 20 (or the third Wednesday of every month) in order for pickup to go smoothly.

For the full agenda, visit: http://www.honolulu.gov/nco/nb6/10/06201010Ag.PDF.

To read last month’s minutes, go to http://www.honolulu.gov/nco/nb6/10/06201009Min.PDF.

If you are a Project Runway junkie like me who has enjoyed following local talent Andy South’s journey this season, make a date to go to Ala Moana this Saturday, October 16. 

Tim Gunn, the Chief Creative Officer of Liz Clairborne Inc., and notorious mentor on the show, will be hosting a fashion show and giving advice on how to make fall’s trends work in Hawaii. If you have some shopping to do, save your receipts. Those who present $150 worth of receipts purchased between October 10 and 16 from Juicy Couture, Lucky Brand Jeans, or Kate Spade get a special treat. At Centerstage on the day of the event, shoppers are invited to the after-party, where you can be professionally photographed with Gunn, sip on tasty cocktails, and score a swag bag full of gotta-have goodies. Thanks, Lei Chic, for the reminder!

Fashion show and after-party
Saturday, October 16
Fashion show starts at 1:00 p.m., after-party from 2:00 p.m. to 4:00 p.m.
Ala Moana Center, Centerstage

The Performing Arts Center of Kapolei (PACK), will be performing the Walt Disney classic “Alice in Wonderland, Jr.”

Performances begin October 21 and will run till October 30. Held at the Kapolei High School Forum, general admission for the play is $6. The event is set for all ages. Reservations are being accepted via email at smcht@mac.com or you can call (808) 224-5314. Tickets are also available at the door.

For more information, visit http://www.packapolei.org.

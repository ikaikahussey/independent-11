The Halloween Week at Sea Life Park will offer an ocean of Halloween fun.

The “spirited” festivities kick off with Halloween arts and crafts in the Adventure Center from Monday, October 25 through Friday, October 29. People of all ages will enjoy lectures, crafts, and animal artifacts daily from 10:00 a.m. to 11:15 a.m. and from 11:30 a.m. to 12:15 p.m.

The thrills and gills continue from 2:00 p.m. to 5:00 p.m. on Saturday, October 30 with Sea Life Park’s Halloween Splash. The family-friendly event will feature Halloween-themed décor as well as a keiki costume contest for a chance to win a Dolphin Encounter valued at $99. Costumed keiki will also enjoy a special treat of candy, Sea Life Park VIP cards, and valuable school supplies, courtesy of Aloha United Way. Admission is just $2 for everyone in costume, children and adults alike.

Sea Life Park is located at 41-202 Kalanianaole Highway, Waimanalo.

For more information, call 259-2500 or visit www.sealifeparkhawaii.com.

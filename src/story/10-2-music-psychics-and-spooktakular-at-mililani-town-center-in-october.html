Town Center of Mililani, 95-1249 Meheula Pkwy., is located in the heart of Mililani and is home to a growing number of tenants including Times Supermarket, Longs Drugs, Consolidated Theaters, Wal-Mart, health care and professional offices, Zippys, and a variety of specialty stores and retail businesses. The shopping complex is designed to grow with the community and, when complete, the 568,000 square foot regional shopping center will be the fourth largest of its kind in the state.

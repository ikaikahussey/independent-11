KAIMUKI—The Kaimuki Neighborhood Board will meet on Wednesday, October 20 at the Queen Liliuokalani Elementary School Cafeteria, located at 3633 Waialae Ave. The meeting starts at 7:15 p.m.

On the agenda, among other items, is Celebrate Kaimuki Kanikapila on November 6. Lydia Chock, president of the Kaimuki Business and Professional Association, will be present to discuss the annual family festivity. Also, an online petition to save Queen Liliuokalani School will be discussed.

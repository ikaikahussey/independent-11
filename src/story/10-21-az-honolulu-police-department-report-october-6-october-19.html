The Honolulu Police Department released the following statistics for the Aiea and Pearl City areas.

For District 3, Sector 3, which includes Aiea and Pearl Kai, a total of 26 calls were made regarding incidents of theft to the HPD from October 6 to October 19. There were also 14 reports of unauthorized entry of a motor vehicle theft.

For District 3, Sector 2, which includes Pearl City and Pacific Palisades, a total of 20 calls were made regarding incidents of theft and 19 incidents of unauthorized entry of a motor vehicle.

The initial classifications are subject to change.

For the full HPD report visit http://www.honolulupd.org/statistics/index.htm.

In search of the perfect costume? Diamond Head Theatre (DHT) can fulfill your Halloween fantasy!

Rentals start at $25 (based on type of costume and duration of rental).

DHT Costume Shop Halloween Hours are from October 18 to November 1, Monday through Saturday from 10:00 a.m. to 6:00 p.m.

Visit their website for costume ideas or call (808) 733-0277, ext. 2.

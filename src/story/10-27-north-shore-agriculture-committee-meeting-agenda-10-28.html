The North Shore Neighborhood Board’s agriculture committee meeting will be held on Thursday, October 28 at 7:00 p.m. The committee will be meeting at the Waialua Community Association, in cottage two across from the Haleiwa Post Office.

On the committee’s agenda is the University of Hawaii’s Waialee Facility, North Shore Sustainable Community Plan’s updated version, and farm news and updates.

The meeting’s agenda can be found below.

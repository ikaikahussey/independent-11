The Kahala Mall will be hosting two Halloween Events this year. 

On Thursday, October 28, from 6:00 p.m. to 9:00 p.m., there will be a Halloween costume contest. Registration will be in front of the Walking company—the contest itself will be at Center Stage.

Then, on Sunday, October 31, participating mall stores will offer safe and early Trick-or-Treating from 3:30 p.m. to 5:00 p.m. Bring the keiki!

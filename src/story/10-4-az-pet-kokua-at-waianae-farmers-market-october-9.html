Bring the pooches and kitties to the “Pet Kokua” presented by the Hawaiian Humane Society at the Waianae Farmers Market at Waianae High School on Saturday, October 9 from 8:00 a.m. to 11:00 a.m.

“Pet Kokua” offers free microchipping, spay/neuter cards, pet food, and other pet related items. Along with the free giveaways, those in attendance will be able to meet animal rescue officers.

Once your done taking care of your pets, enjoy the farmer’s market featuring fresh produce and home made goods. There will also be children’s artwork on display by students from Makaha Elementary, Maili Bible School, and Maili Elementary.

The Keiki Care Center of Hawaii (KCCOH) is holding a Scholastic Book Fair from October 6 to 8. The book fair will be held in the multi-purpose room at their new school on 757 Hoomalu Street near the Pearl City Shopping Center.

MyPearlCity.com’s Barry Villamil reports that proceeds from the KCCOH Scholastic Book Fair directly benefit the school by enhancing the learning experience, educational opportunities, and growth for both students and teachers.

Along with the book sale, there will be organic potted plants and Cookie Corner Cookies at the book fair.

Come to support KCCOH Scholastic Book Fair and meet CEO and owner of KCCOH, Cheryl R. Cudiamat and her staff, teachers, students, parents, and volunteers within the Pearl City community.

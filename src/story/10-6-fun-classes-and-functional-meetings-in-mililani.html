As a town focused on family community, Mililani Town provides many classes, gatherings, and meetings to fit the needs of its members. Whether you’re looking for a fun or functional activity, it’s likely either one’s going on in Mililani. Here are some current classes and meetings going on through the Mililani Town Association.

Aloha Aquatics Association
Check swim schedule at Recreation Center II pool
Contact: Ken Fukada at (808) 282-6626

Boy Scout Troop #164
Where: Recreation Center VI Room I
When: Thursdays from 7:00 p.m. to 9:00 p.m.
Contact: Frank Storch (808) 625-0880 or visit http://troop164.ods.org

Disabled American Veterans
Where: Recreation Center 6
When: Every third Tuesday from 7:00 p.m. to 9:00 p.m.
Contact: Alan Catz at (808) 625-3638

Girl Scout Council of Hawaii
Where: Rainbow Service Unit—Mililani
Registering new girl scouts the first Tuesday of every month at Recreation Center VI from 6:30 p.m. to 7:00 p.m.
Contact: Cassandra Pang (808) 779-5319

Judo
Where: Recreation Center I
When: Tuesdays and Thursdays from 5:00 p.m. to 7:00 p.m.
Contact: Clarence Nishi at (808) 623-3777

Jump rope general class, ages eight and up
Where: Recreation Center III
When: General class on Wednesdays from 3:00 p.m. to 5:30 p.m.
Contact: Ron Stephens at (808) 625-5629

Leeward Dog Obedience
Where: Recreation Center I
When: April and August every third Wednesday from 7:30 p.m. to 9:00 p.m.
Contact: Phyllis McFadden at (808) 623-6280 or visit http://www.ltch.org

Mililani Chess Club
Where: Recreation Center VI Room II & III
When: Thursdays from 5:30 p.m. to 9:00 p.m.
Contact: Randy Prothero at (808) 384-5645

Mililani Garden Club
Where: Recreation Center VI
When: September through May every third Thursday from 9:30 a.m. to 12:00 p.m.
Contact: Call (808) 625-8789 or (808) 625-6778 or visit www.mililanigardenclub.com

Mililani Lions Club
Where: Recreation Center I
When: First, second and fourth Wednesday of every month from 7:30 p.m. to 9:00 p.m.
Contact: Michael Lau at (808) 225-0175

Neighborhood board meeting #25
Where: Recreation Center III
When: Fourth Wednesday of every month from 7:30 p.m. to 10:00 p.m.
Contact: Dick Poirier at (808) 623-2259

Neighborhood Board meeting #35
Where: Mililani Mauka Elementary School
When: Third Tuesday of every month at 7:00 p.m.
Contact: Dean Hazama at (808) 226-7430

M.T.A. Board of Directors meeting
Where: Recreation Center III, Conference Room
When: Third Wednesday of every month at 7:00 p.m.
Contact: Administration office at (808) 623-7300 or Recreation Center III at (808) 623-7110
Meetings are open to M.T.A. members. To address a concern, members must submit a written request. They will then be referred to a respective standing committee of the board.

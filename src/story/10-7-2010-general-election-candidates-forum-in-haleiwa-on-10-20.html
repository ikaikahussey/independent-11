The Waialua Community Association is hosting a 2010 general election candidates forum on Wednesday, October 20 at 6:00 p.m. The forum will be held at the Historic Haleiwa Gym across from the post office in Haleiwa.

Doors will open at 5:45 p.m. and attendees will have the opportunity to visit information tables with campaign literature from House and Senate candidates. Questions provided by the community will be answered in the moderated forum. To submit a question to the forum, send an email to wca96712@hawaii.rr.com.

For more information, call Nicole at (808) 637-4606 or email wca96712@hawaii.rr.com.

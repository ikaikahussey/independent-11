On Tuesday September 7, the Waianae Coast Neighborhood Board met for their monthly regular meeting. Held at Waianae Neighborhood Community Center, eight board members were in attendance for the 7:00 p.m. meeting.

During the meeting, Keith Pine was appointed to the board receiving five votes over Jack De Feo’s three votes. Also, Mark Dannog introduced himself to the board and people in attendance. Dannog is the student member of the Board of Education and also a current senior at Waianae High School. The board applauded his introduction and presentation, noting he is “shinning example” for the community.

For full listing of the regular meeting minutes visit http://www.honolulu.gov/nco/nb24/10/24sepmin.htm.

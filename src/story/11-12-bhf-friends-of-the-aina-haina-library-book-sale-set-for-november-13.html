AINA HAINA—A holiday book sale will be conducted by the Friends of the Aina Haina Library on Saturday, November 13 at the Aina Haina Public Library. The five-hour book sale happens from 10:00 a.m. to 3:00 p.m.

Holiday shoppers will find specially-priced items such as gift quality books, collectibles, and DVDs for sale in front of the library. A selection of books for children and adults, as well as CDs and VHS tapes will be available for purchase in the Meeting Room. Prices will begin at $2.00 for hard cover books and $1.00 for paperbacks.

Proceeds from the sale will benefit the library by helping to purchase books and other materials. Anyone interested in joining the Friends of the Aina Haina Library should leave their name and telephone number at the Library’s Reference Desk.

The Aina Haina Public Library is located at 5246 Kalanianaole Highway in the Aina Haina Shopping Center. For more information, call the library at (808) 377-2456.

The Mililani/Waipio/Melemanu Neighborhood Board will be holding an agenda setting meeting on Tuesday, November 16 at 7:00 p.m. where board members will meet at Jack in the Box in Mililani.

The The Mililani/Waipio/Melemanu Neighborhood Board usually holds meetings once each month, with the occasion meeting recess for a particular month.

The board is made up of 23 community members.

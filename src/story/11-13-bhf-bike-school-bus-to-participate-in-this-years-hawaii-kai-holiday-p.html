The Bike School Bus, which aims to make Hawaii a safer place for biking, will be participating in this year’s Hawaii Kai Holiday Parade sponsored by the Hawaii Kai Lions Club. The parade will start at 10:00 a.m. on Saturday, November 27, near the skateboard park at Lunalilo Home Road and Hawaii Kai Drive. The parade route is approximately 1.5 miles and ends at Koko Marina Shopping Center. 

Bicyclists interested in participating should contact Natalie Iwasa, aka Bicycle Mom, to register. Helmets will be required during the parade. Natalie may be reached at (808) 396-7721.

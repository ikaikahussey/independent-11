The Mililani Mauka/Launani Valley Neighborhood Board will be holding their regular meeting on Tuesday, November 16 at the Mililani Mauka Elementary School. The meeting, which is set to begin at 7:00 p.m., has several community issues on the agenda.

Monthly reports from the Honolulu Fire Department, Honolulu Police Department, and the Board of Water Supply will be provided near the beginning of the meeting. Council member Donovan Dela Cruz’s representative Reed Matsuura will provide a report from their office as well as the mayor’s representative Collins Lam. Reports from Castle and Cooke and the Oahu Arts Center about the Patsy T. Mink Central Oahu Regional Park will be given to attendees.

Residents will also have the opportunity to express their own concerns to the board.

The board will also be on recess for December and will meet again on January 18, 2011.

The full agenda follows:

It’s back! The Kaimuki Business and Professional Association (KBPA) and KBPA Foundation present the 64th Annual Kaimuki Christmas Parade along Waialae Avenue on Thursday, December 2, starting at 6:00 p.m. This year’s Grand Marshal will be Kaimuki Christian Church. The parade will feature area schools, businesses, restaurants, and politicians. And, as always, expect Santa to make an appearance!

For more information, visit http://www.kaimukihawaii.com/d/c/kaimuki-business-professional-association.html.

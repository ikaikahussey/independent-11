AINA HAINA—The Kuliouou/Kalani Iki Neighborhood Board will be meeting on Thursday, November 4 at 7:00 p.m. at the Aina Haina Library, located at 5246 Kalanianaole Highway.

Included on the agenda are updates on the renovations to the Aina Haina Shopping Center and the sewer project on Kalanianaole Highway. There will also be a motion to fill one vacancy on the board, for Subdistrict 6 (Aina Haina).

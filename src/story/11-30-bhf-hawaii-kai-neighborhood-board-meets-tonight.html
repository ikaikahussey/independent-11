The Hawaii Kai Neighborhood Board will convene on Tuesday, November 30 at 7:00 p.m. in the Hahaione Elementary School Cafeteria, located at 595 Pepeekeo Street. 

Included on the agenda, are possible school closures in the Kaiser Complex as well as the desirability of keeping Kamilonui Valley zoned for and in active agricultural use.

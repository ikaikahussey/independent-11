The McCully/Moiiliili Neighborhood Board will meet tonight, November 4, at 6:30 p.m. at Washington Middle School, located at 1633 S. King Street. Come and discuss many resolutions on making government more accountable in the 2011 session.

McCully/Moiliili Neighborhood Board Meeting
Thursday, November 4 from 6:30 p.m. to 8:30 p.m.
Washington Middle School Cafeteria, 1633 S. King Street

The official meeting agenda follows below.


RULES OF SPEAKING:  Anyone wishing to speak must raise their hand; and after recognition address their comments to the Chair. Comments must relate to the pending agenda item. Speakers are encouraged to keep their comments under one (1) minute, and those giving reports are encouraged to keep their reports to less than three (3) minutes.  Please silence all electronic devices.  Presentations will begin at 7:30 p.m.
NOTES:  The Board may take action on any agenda item.  As required by the State Sunshine Law (HRS, Chapter 92) specific issues not listed on this agenda cannot be voted on, unless properly added to the agenda.  Only two (2) turns to speak, including Board members, of one (1) minute each.  Times listed are for time management and are not to be considered as absolutes.

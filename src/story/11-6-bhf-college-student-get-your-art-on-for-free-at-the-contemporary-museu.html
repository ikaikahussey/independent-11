This Saturday, November 6 is College Day at The Contemporary Museum (TCM). Free admission is extended to all students, faculty and staff (with a valid College ID) at TCM in Makiki Heights.

TCM is located at 2411 Makiki Heights Drive and open from 10:00 a.m. to 4:00 p.m..

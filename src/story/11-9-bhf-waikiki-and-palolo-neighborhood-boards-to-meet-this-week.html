The Waikiki neighborhood board will meet at 7:00 p.m. on Tuesday, November 9 at the Waikiki Community Center, located at 310 Paoakalani Avenue.  Included in the agenda are updates on the transit system and electric vehicle power stations. For the complete agenda, click here.

The Palolo neighborhood board will meet at 7:00 p.m. on Wednesday, November 10 in the Palolo Elementary School Cafeteria, located at 2106 10th Avenue.  On the agenda, among other items, is the resolution opposing the closure of Queen Liliuokalani Elementary School.

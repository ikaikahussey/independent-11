Coffee Talk is hosting a “Game Night” featuring the Hawaii designed board game “Waimea Wipeout” on December 16 from 7:00 p.m. until 9:30 p.m. Come and experience surfing’s “first true board game” with the creators on-hand to to teach you the ropes. Visit the Waimea Wipeout website for details on the game.

Coffee Talk is located at 3601 Waialae Avenue in Kaimuki.  For more information, call (808) 737-7444.

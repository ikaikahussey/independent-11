The McCully/Moiliili Neighborhood Board meets at Washington Middle School’s Cafeteria, located at 1633 S. King Street, from 6:30 p.m. to 8:30 p.m.  Various resolutions and capital improvement projects will be discussed; for the complete agenda, click here.

Just in time for the holidays, the Vers Christmas bash is the perfect time and place to pick up last minute stocking-stuffers and hostess gifts. Gather your friends and stop by to eat pupus while you shop, meet Vers artists Roxy and Matt, and score some locally-made goodies. Vers has several new designs of organic tees for men and women, plus the painted hats of Vers are back by popular demand. Vers merchandise will be 10 percent off and for this night only, hats will be $20.

Vers Christmas Bash
Monday, December 20 from 5:00 p.m. to 7:00 p.m.
Drift Boutique, 3434 Waialae Ave
(across the street from Town restaurant)

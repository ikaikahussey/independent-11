Wahoo’s Fish Taco Hawaii is now in Kahala at 4614 Kilauea Ave., right next to Olive Tree Cafe and the post office.

On December 21, Wahoo’s will celebrate its grand opening with a Taco Tuesday event from 5:00 p.m. until midnight. $2.00 Primos, $2.00 Tacos, and live music by Local Uprising are highlights of the evening. Also, one percent of sales all day will benefit Malama Maunalua and Surfrider Foundation. 

Wahoo’s Fish Taco Hawaii in Kahala grand opening party
Tuesday, December 21 from 5:00 p.m. to 12:00 a.m.
4614 Kilauea Ave.
Park at the Kahala post office.

Kamea Hadar’s latest show of hauntingly beautiful portraits, Human Nature, will debut at the new Waikiki Edition Hotel (in both its Lobby Bar and Crazybox nightclub) on Wednesday, December 29. Festivities start at 6:00 p.m. and include light pupus, cocktails, and free valet. 

Hadar, who currently lives in Honolulu, is an accomplished artist whose oeuvre spans a wide range of media and reflects his dynamic, international upbringing. He current collection will be featured in the premiere 2011 issue of Innov8 Magazine (inflight zine for Go! airlines). 

The Waikiki Edition Hotel is located at 1775 Ala Moana Blvd.

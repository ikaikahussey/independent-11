Forever 21, the LA-based retailer of trendy women’s, men’s, and children’s clothing and accessories, will unveil its new 42,520 square-foot store at the Royal Hawaiian Center with a grand opening on Sunday, December 26, starting at 10:00 a.m. The tri-level, Flagship store is located near Cheesecake Factory in Building C, on the Diamond Head end of the Center.

Grand opening festivities on December 26 include Forever 21 gift card giveaways, special discounts, and free lockets with $40 purchase (while supplies last). There will also be a DJ spinning music, Forever 21 fashion stylists available for appointments, and a raffle drawing.

Free parking is available at Royal Hawaiian Center with validation (no minimum purchase required). For more information, visit www.Forever21.com, Facebook at “Forever 21,” or Twitter at Forever21_tweet.

Kapiolani Community College (KCC) will host a Pau Hana Jam on its Great Lawn tonight, Friday, December 3 from 5:30 p.m. until 9:30 p.m. Featured acts include Black Square and The Gods of HiFi. Admission is free, and all ages are welcome. There will lots of free parking, too. Hot food will be available for purchase.

For more information, call (808) 734-9000 or (808) 734-9576.

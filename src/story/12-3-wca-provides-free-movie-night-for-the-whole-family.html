A free family movie night is planned for Friday, December 17 when the Waialua Community Association (WCA) will show the 80s comedy classic The God’s Must Be Crazy. Admission is free and drinks and popcorn will be available for purchase.

The Waialua Community Association is located in the “old gym” across from the Haleiwa Post Office. The movie night is an extension of the WCA’s mission to serve the North Shore community with “meaningful, educational, and recreational programs.”

One of the oldest community associations in Hawaii, the WCA was established in 1934 and continues to serve as a gathering place where children, teens, and seniors are encouraged to engage in meaningful social activities that stimulate and support individuals and community.

For more information, call (808) 637-4606.

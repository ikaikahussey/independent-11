The Diamond Head/Kapahulu/Saint Louis Neighborhood Board will meet on Thursday, February 10 at 7:00 p.m. at the Ala Wai Club House, located at 404 Kapahulu Ave.  Board business to be discussed includes a traffic signal at St. Louis Drive and Dole Street and a street lighting project in St. Louis Heights.

The North Shore Neighborhood Board will gather for its monthly meeting on Tuesday, February 22 at the John Kalili Surf Center located at Haleiwa Alii Beach Park. The meeting will begin at 7:00 p.m.

The board will address the filling of a board vacancy for the sub-district of Mokuleia. The board is asking residents residing in the district to volunteer to fill the position. Applicants must submit proper documents for proof of residency.

Monthly reports from the Honolulu Fire Department, the Honolulu Police Department, Mayor Peter Carlisle’s Representative, the Board of Water Supply, and Councilmember Ernie Martin will be addressed at the start of the meeting.

Board business includes a presentation by Kalani Fronda of Kamehameha Schools on the Culture Center at Kamehameha Highway/Iliohu Street.

The complete meeting agenda is below.


Rules of Speaking: Anyone wishing to speak is asked to raise their hand, and when recognized by the Chair, to address comments to the Chair. Speakers are encouraged to keep their comments under 3 minutes, and those giving reports are urged to keep their reports less than 3 minutes. Please silence all electronic devices.

Note: The Board may take action on any agenda item. As required by the State Sunshine Law (HRS 92), specific issues not noted on this agenda cannot be voted on, unless added to the agenda.

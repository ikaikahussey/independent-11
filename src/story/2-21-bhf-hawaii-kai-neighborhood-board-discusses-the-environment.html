The Hawaii Kai Neighborhood Board will meet Tuesday, February 22 at 7:00 p.m. at the Hahaione Elementary School Cafeteria, located at 595 Pepeekeo Street. Hawaii Kai Hui will present information on wetlands, while Malama Maunalua’s presentation, “Every Drop Counts,” concerns the next stage of the Maunalua Bay Restoration Project.

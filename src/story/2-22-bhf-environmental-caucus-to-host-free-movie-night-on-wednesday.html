MAKIKI—The Environmental Caucus of the Democratic Party of Hawaii is holding a general membership meeting and movie night on Wednesday, February 23 at 6:00 p.m. at St. Clement’s Episcopal Church Auditorium, located at 1515 Wilder Ave.

Following the meeting, at 7:30 p.m., will be a screening Two Angry Moms, a documentary about two women who set out to change the way their schools provide food to their children by creating school gardens. There will also be an informative and inspirational “How to Guide” for how our community can to do the same. Admission to view the film is free and open to anyone in the community.

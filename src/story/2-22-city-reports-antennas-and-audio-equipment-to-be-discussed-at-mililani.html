The Mililani/Waipio/Melemanu Neighborhood Board will hold their regular monthly meeting on Wednesday, February 23 at Mililani Recreation Center III.

Board members and residents will gather to discuss a variety of community issues including monthly reports from the Honolulu Fire Department, the Honolulu Police Department, and the Board of Water Supply.

New business includes a motion in support of the proposed AT&T Wireless antenna at the Mililani High School Football Field, a resolution in support of regulating after-market car audio equipment, and a resolution in support of restoring state vector control positions and services.

The complete meeting agenda is below.

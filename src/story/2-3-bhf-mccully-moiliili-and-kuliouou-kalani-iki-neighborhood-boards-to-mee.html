The McCully-Moiliili Neighborhood Board will meet Thursday, February 3 from 6:30 p.m. to 8:30 p.m. at the Washington Middle School Cafeteria, located at 1633 S. King Street. Included in the many topics to be discussed is the impact of the landfill’s closure on the ever-problematic issue of bulky Item pick up.

On the same evening, Ron Lockwood, Chair of McCully-Moiliili Neighborhood Board, will visit the Kuliouou-Kalani Iki Neighborhood Board meeting to make a presentation on the Totally Against Graffiti (TAG) Team Event in McCully. The Kuliouou-Kalani Iki Neighborhood Board meets at 7:00 p.m. in the Aina Haina Public Library, located at 5246 Kalanianaole Hwy.

KAIMUKI—Beginning last week, road work consisting primarily of reconstructing and resurfacing the asphalt roadways in the Kaimuki/Kapahulu/Diamond Head neighborhoods has been underway and is expected to last 300 calendar days.

Remember to observe all posted signs and expect lane closures and delays. The primary areas affected will be makai of Waialae Avenue, between Kapahulu and 18th Avenue.

The Koolauloa Neighborhood Board No. 28 will be holding their regular meeting on Thursday, February 10 at the Hau’ula Community Center.

On the agenda are City monthly reports from the Honolulu Fire Department, Honolulu Police Department, the mayor’s representative, Councilmember Ernie Martin, and the Board of Water Supply.

Board business includes: filling of board vacancy, the Capitol Improvement Project (CIP) suggestion submissions to City and County of Honolulu, a support letter addressing Kokololio Park gates, a support letter for Kahuku Medical Center and a discussion on the mental and emotional well being of the youth of Koolauloa.

The complete agenda is below.

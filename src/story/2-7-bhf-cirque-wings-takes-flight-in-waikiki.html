Looking for a unique date spot, family event, or a fun night out? The whole family can enjoy the magic and wonder of Cirque Wings on Saturday, February 12 at the Waikiki Shell Amphitheater. There is nothing else like it on Oahu.  Audiences of all ages will witness the amazing performers portraying the history of flight as they effortlessly glide 30 feet high in the air displaying stunning aerial and acrobatic feats.

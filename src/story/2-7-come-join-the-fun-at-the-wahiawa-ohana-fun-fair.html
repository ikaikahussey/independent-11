Come join the fun at the Wahiawa Ohana Fun Fair, open to children, parents, friends, and anyone who wants to attend the family oriented event.

Festivities include displays, vendors, entertainment, and activities for the keiki all happening on Saturday, February 19.

The fair will will be held at the Dot’s Restaurant parking lot and Banquet Hall.

For more information, contact the Wahiawa Community and Business Association at (808) 621-6531.

Wahiawa Ohana Fun Fair
Saturday, February 19
Dot’s Restaurant parking lot and Banquet Hall
130 Mango Street

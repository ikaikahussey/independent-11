Hawaii Fashion Incubator (hifi) is hosting a spring pop-up fashion event in Ala Moana from March 18 through March 27. The “incubator” will be located on Level 1 of the Ala Moana mall, near Old Navy. 

Featured are fashion classes and workshops, designer showcases and trunk shows, crafting projects for the whole family, and the COOP Local Design Boutique. Project Runway‘s Andy South will give a runway show on March 24.

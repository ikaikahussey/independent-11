The North Shore Neighborhood Board will be holding their regular monthly meeting on Tuesday, March 22 at the John Kalili Surf Center in Haleiwa.

On the agenda are a variety of issues, including City monthly reports from Honolulu Fire Department, Honolulu Police Department, Mayor Peter Carlisle’s representative Jim Fulton, the Board of Water Supply, and Councilmember Ernie Martin.

Board business also includes a presentation on the Papahanaumoku Monument, the election of Board Secretary, and discussion of an in-vessel composting project.

The complete meeting agenda is below.

Tuesday, March 22, 2011
John Kalili Surf Center – Hale’iwa Ali’i Beach Park
66-167 Hale’iwa Road

Speakers are encouraged to keep their comments under 3 minutes, and those giving reports are urged to keep their reports less than 3 minutes. Please silence all electronic devices.

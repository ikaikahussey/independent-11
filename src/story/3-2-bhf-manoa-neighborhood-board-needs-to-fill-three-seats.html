The Manoa Neighborhood Board will meet Wednesday, March 2 from 7:00 p.m. at the Manoa Elementary School Cafetorium, located at 3155 Manoa Road.

Items to be discussed include the appointment of three vacant board seats and the corresponding election process. As always, the public is encouraged to attend.

The Kuliouou-Kalani Iki Neighborhood Board will meet Thursday, March 3 at 7:00 p.m. in the Aina Haina Public Library, located at 5246 Kalanianaole Highway. The board will be discussing a resolution regarding the rezoning of Kuliouou Elementary School Students. As always, the public is encouraged to attend.

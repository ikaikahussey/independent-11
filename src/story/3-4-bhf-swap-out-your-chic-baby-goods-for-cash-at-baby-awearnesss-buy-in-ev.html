Are you looking to get rid of toys, books, and cloth diapers? Start your spring cleaning and get cash or store credit at Baby Awearness’s Baby A Exchange (BAX) Buy-In event set for Saturday March 5 from 10:00 a.m. to 4:00 p.m. 

There will also be a special BAX Bargain Basket filled with goodies ($3.50 or less) as well as a donation drive of any books and toys not accepted for resale. Storytelling in English, Spanish, and Japanese will help keep the little ones entertained while you shop! 

Baby Awearness is located at 2752 Woodlawn Drive, Ste 5-209.

For more information, visit their website or call (808) 988-0010.

CNN reports on the five-year anniversary of the tragic flooding that occurred in New Orleans caused by Hurricane Katrina:

“Five years ago Sunday, the water rushed in, the lights went out and for thousands of Gulf Coast residents nothing was ever the same. The milestone was marked by vigils, tears and reflection on what was, what came after, what still remains to be done and what, if anything, we have learned from Hurricane Katrina.”

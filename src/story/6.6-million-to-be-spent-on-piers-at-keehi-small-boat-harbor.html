HONOLULU—The Department of Land and Natural Resources completed a new dock and broke ground for two new projects at Keehi Small Boat Harbor in July. The new dock replaced the old Pier 100. The groundbreaking kicked off the installation of two new docks to replace Piers 600, 900, and 1000.

The ongoing replacement of the piers, which is scheduled for completion in 2011, will bring 96 slips back into service for Hawaii’s boating community.

Another 32 slips at the harbor’s Pier 300 are presently in the design phase.

Over $4.1 million has been spent to make improvements at Keehi Small Boat Harbor and an additional $2.5 million will be spent on the new Piers 600 and 900.

College speakers from around the United States are flocking to Kapolei High School to inform Hurricane seniors about their prospective schools. For the remainder for the month of September, there will be ongoing college presentations for Kapolei students to attend.

Some schools that will be speaking with students this month include: Gonzaga University, Western Oregon University, University of Washington, Tokai University, and Holy Names University. Students interested in attending presentations sign up in Kapolei High School room G208.

For a full listing of college speakers, go to http://www.edline.net/pages/Kapolei_High_School/Calendar.

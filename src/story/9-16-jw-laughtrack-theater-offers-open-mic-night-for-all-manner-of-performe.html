Laughtrack Theater on Bethel Street holds an open mic night on the third Thursday of each month, welcoming all manner of comedians, musicians, poets, and writers. The event is free for performers.

Open Mic Night makes it possible for both the artist and the audience to extend and enhance their experience of the performing arts. This project gives local entertainers a place to showcase and develop their skills and talents, and gives art-loving audiences an opportunity to hear and see some of Honoluluâ€™s newest talents.

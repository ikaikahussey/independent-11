WAIANAE—On Tuesday, September 21, Neighborhood Board 36, representing Nanakuli and Maili, will hold its regular community meeting.

Held at Nanaikapono Elementart Cafeteria, the board will review business presented from last month’s meeting including a request of the board to support writing a letter of complaint about the past and current construction work and the unfair consumer claims process by Okada Trucking Company regarding its work and project on the Farrington Highway corridor in Nanakuli and Ko Olina.

The full regular meeting agenda is listed below:

Neighborhood Board 34, which covers Kapolei, Makakilo, and Honokai Hale, will hold its regular meeting on Wednesday, September 22 at 7:00 p.m.. The board will host representative Patrick Sequirant from Castle and Cooke Hawaii to present an update on housing development Village at Kapolei and future plans for the housing community.


The full regular meeting agenda is listed below:

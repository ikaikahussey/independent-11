On Tuesday September 28, Neighborhood Board 21, representing the Pearl City area, will hold its regular community meeting.

After recessing for a month, the board will host several different presentations for those in attendance at the Waiau District Park pavilion. Presenters include the Waimano Home “State Laboratory” Swine Flu Pandemic, representatives from RMY Construction company working on the Waimalu Sewer, and Breene Harimoto, who will speak on behalf of Hawaii’s Children First organization.


The full regular meeting agend is listed below:

The Hawaii Kai Neighborhood Board will meet tonight at 7:00 p.m. at Hahaione Elementary’s cafeteria. This meeting primarily serves as a candidate forum for candidates seeking seats in the Legislature (Senate District 8 and 25, House District 18) and the State Board of Education from Oahu. The forum will be moderated by Beth-Ann Kozlovich.

For this week’s complete agenda, visit http://www.honolulu.gov/nco/nb1/10/01201009Ag.pdf

To read the minutes from the August 31 meeting, visit http://www.honolulu.gov/nco/nb1/10/01201008Min.PDF.

Shana Okuda, a resume writer and copy editor, will conduct a 90-minute workshop titled “How to Write a Resume” on Thursday, September 30 at 6:00 p.m.

Learn how to prepare your resume according to the job you are seeking. Participants are asked to bring a rough draft of their resume, a draft cover letter, notebook, and a pen or pencil. The workshop will include a question-and-answer session and is suitable for ages 16 and older. Registration is not required. The workshop is free.

For more information, call (808) 483-7333.

Kaimuki High School is hosting a blood drive this Wednesday, September 29, from 7:00 a.m. until 12:45 p.m. Oahu residents can help save three lives in about an hour by giving blood at various Blood Bank of Hawaii blood drives around the island.  Every day in Hawaii, hundreds of patients count on volunteer blood donors for their very survival. To donate you must be in good health, be 18 years of age or older, weigh 110 pounds or more and provide a valid photo ID. If you are not already registered, appointments are suggested. 

Call (808) 845-9966 for more information.

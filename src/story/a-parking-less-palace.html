KHON quotes Ward:

“Look a the those ugly cars. Is this a palace or is this a parking lot? Or a used car lot for Hawaiian kingdoms?” says Rep. Gene Ward, (R) Hawaii Kai, Kalama Valley.

Ward says it shouldn’t be a question, “I think it’s apparent to everyone that we honor the Hawaiian tradition and culture, why don’t we honor Iolani Palace by getting those cars off.”

Parking is already at a premium in downtown Honolulu. Access to the Capitol building is already very difficult, so why make it harder?

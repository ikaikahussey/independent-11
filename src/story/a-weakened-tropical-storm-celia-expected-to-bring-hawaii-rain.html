The season’s second, third, and fourth named storms of the eastern Pacific hurricane season formed in rapid succession off Mexico’s west coast, Earthweek reports. Residents of Hawaii were hoping remnants of the weakened Tropical Storm Celia would bring some much-needed rainfall to the state within about a week’s time.

Aloha Tower Marketplace will be joining in with other Oahu Fourth of July celebrations on Saturday, July 3 from 3:00 p.m. to 10:00 p.m., featuring a variety of keiki activities such as a bounce house and face painting, and free admission to the grand opening of the Aloha Lounge and Gallery. The evening’s activities will also include musical performances by the keiki group Emke, local jazz ensemble AIR and local rock groups Prevail and I Digress.

The evening’s main attraction, a spectacular fireworks display gets begins at 8:45 p.m.

Validated and valet parking are available at Aloha Tower Marketplace for reasonable rates. Additional off-site parking is available at the Topa Financial Center, Harbor Court and Harbor Square.

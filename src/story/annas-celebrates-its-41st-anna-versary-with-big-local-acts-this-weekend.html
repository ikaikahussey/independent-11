Anna’s (formerly known as Anna Bananas, 2440 S. Beretania St.) is celebrating 41 years in Moiliili this weekend on Friday, July 9 and Saturday, July 10 from 9:00 p.m. to 12:00 a.m. Cover is $5 for 21 and up.

Performances on Friday include Shopping List, Painted Highways, Falcon Lord, Mano Kane, and special guest DJ Sergio Iglesias from San Francisco.

Performances on Saturday include Black Sapote, The Fabulists, Circuit Creature, Invisible Monsters, Sabrina, and DJ Cash.

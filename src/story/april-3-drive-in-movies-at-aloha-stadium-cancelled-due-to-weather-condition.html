Due to the weather, the outdoor drive-in movie planned for Saturday, April 3 at the Aloha Stadium has been canceled. The first of six “free” drive-in movies in the parking lot of Aloha Stadium on Friday, March 19 was a huge success and the public was eagerly awaiting the second showing, which was to feature The Blind Side. Due to the high wind and rainy conditions, the screen will not be able to stay inflated and therefore attendees would not be able to view the movie adequately. A new date will be announced soon.

For more information, visit http://www.alohastadiumswapmeet.net.

Pride At Work Hawaii’s legislative action committee will host an envelope-stuffing, pizza party on Wednesday, August 11 from 5:30 p.m. at Local 5 to send primary election issue surveys to all candidates for governor, lieutenant governor, State House, and State Senate. The surveys will ask candidates’ positions on LGBT and workers rights, affordable housing, women’s reproductive rights, maintaining government services, health care, and more.

Pride At Work Hawaii will then share the results via email, their website, and through the local LGBT rights network.

RSVP by emailing prideatworkhawaii@hawaiiantel.net.

Local 5 is located at 1516 S. King, next to Sanders Piano and ewa of the corner of Kalakaua Avenue. Enter the building from the back (Young St.) entrance; ring the bell to be let in.

Pride At Work Hawaii advocates for full equality for LGBITQ workers in their workplaces and their unions, and works to build mutual support between the labor movement and the LGBITQ community. Pride At Work Hawaii (an affiliate of the Hawaii State AFL-CIO) is a chapter of Pride At Work, a constituency group of the AFL-CIO.

Industry leaders and experts will take a prospective look at Hawaii’s economy and future recovery at a forum titled “Recovery and Transformation: Straight Talk on Rebuilding.”

Four panel discussions put together by Thinktech Hawaii will include members of Hawaii’s business community from various industries who will talk about identifying challenges, building best practices for Hawaii, making a plan to shape our feature, as well as sharing the vision of Hawaii’s new leaders. Each panel discussion will be followed by a public question-and-answer session.

Keynote speakers will include Robbie Alm, CEO of HECO, Peter Ho, president and CEO of Bank of Hawaii, Mayor Billy Kenoi and Janet Liang, president of Kaiser Permanente Hawaii.

For more information, call (808) 282-4398.

“Recovery and Transformation: Straight Talk on Rebuilding”
Wednesday, August 25 from 8:00 a.m. to 1:30 p.m.

900 Fort Street Mall #2000

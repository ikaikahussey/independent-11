Hawaii Electric Light Company, the Big Island electricity utility, is petitioning the Public Utilities Commission for 50 additional megawatts of geothermal energy. The request is a revision to an earlier draft proposal to the PUC.

The utility company has issued a Geothermal Request for Proposals to supply the energy, which is available here.

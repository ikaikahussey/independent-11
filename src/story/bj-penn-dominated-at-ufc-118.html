Hawaii hero BJ “The Prodigy” Penn was dominated by Frankie Edgar in a rematch for the Lightweight Championship at UFC 118 in Boston, Edgar’s hometown, this weekend. Penn had lost his championship to the ground-and-pound of Edgar just four months earlier in Abu Dhabi. While fans were hoping for a rejuvenated Penn to take the belt back, it was all Edgar whose quickness on his feet and aggression on the ground outclassed the Hilo son.

BJ Penn has been confirmed to participate in the Big Boys & MMA Hawaii Expo. All BJ Penn merchandise will be on sale at the event.

Besides live cage fights and a grappling tournament, exciting displays include fast cars and custom motorcycles, electronics and gadgets show, live musical entertainment from Anuhea, The Throwdowns and YOZA, tattoo art contest, Miss MMA Hawaii contest, beer garden, cigar lounge, American bully dog show, special guest appearance from UFC Champion Rashad Evans, a bikini contest, lingerie show and more.

“The Big Boys & MMA Hawaii Expo, Hawaii’s Baddest Display of Big Boys and Their Toys” is a monster expo that will feature more than 100 vendors showcasing products and services geared toward men, but women won’t want to miss out either. It is being held Friday and Saturday, June 11 and 12 at the Neal S. Blaisdell Center’s Exhibition Hall. Expo doors open both days from 11:00 a.m. to 10:00 p.m. For more information visit www.hawaiibigboysshow.com or www.mmahawaii.com.

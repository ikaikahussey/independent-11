The Hawaii Board of Education is holding a special meeting on Thursday, April 15 at 7:30 p.m. and Friday, April 16 at 8:00 a.m. at the Queen Liliuokalani Building, 1390 Miller Street Room 404.


The agenda includes hearing public testimony and petitions as well as discussing and decision-making on:

* Hawaii public charter schools probation and revocation procedures

* Compulsory attendance exceptions

* Public summer school tuitions

* Merit Appeals Board

* Definitions in proposed chapter 200.4, Hawaii Administrative Rules

* General policies governing library services

* Library cards

* Loan of circulating library material

* Circulation fines, fees, and charges

* General procedures for library services and the use and loan of library material

* Use and loan of audiovisual equipment, software, microcomputer equipment, and non-print material

* Management and use of public library facilities

* Selection and acquisition of library material

* Civil rights complaint procedure

* Library services

* Library enhanced services

* Library programs

* Donations to public libraries

* Administrative review process

* Administrative practice and procedure

For more information, visit http://www.boe.k12.hi.us.

Last year, “Bodies ... The Exhibition” caused a stir in Hawaii. The exhibition featured cadavers from China posed and dissected for paying viewers. A backlash led Hawaii lawmakers to put forward resolutions to prevent such an exhibit from returning. Now, as “Bodies ... The Exhibition makes its way to St. Louis, Missouri, the exhibit’s medical director is saying he is confident the cadavers are not those of Chinese political dissidents. Associated Press reports.

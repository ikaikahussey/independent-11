Bogart’s Cafe makes my favorite breakfast bagel sandwich. It comes with your choice of toasted bagel, melted cheese, spinach, a meet choice, tomato, and egg. But I order it without meat or egg, and add their mac nut pesto cream cheese which makes it even better. Whenever I can, I drive all the way to the end of town for my custom breakfast sandwich and an iced latte, the only breakfast I actually go out for. Bogart’s is affordable, and offers eat in or take out service. Anytime before noon can be pretty crowded though, so it’s a good idea to call in your order before you get there.

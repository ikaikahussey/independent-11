WAHIAWA—California Avenue in Wahiawa will be repaired and resurfaced from its east end to Kamehameha Highway over the next 14 months.

The $7.2 million project includes demolition and removal of existing pavements, curbs, and/or gutters; installation of aggregate base course, geogrid and woven geotextile, asphalt concrete base, and asphalt concrete pavement; installation of grade adjustment curb; construction of new concrete slabs, curbs and gutters; tree and root trimming and pruning as necessary; adjustment of street monuments and manholes; restoration of all pavement loop detectors, striping, markings and markers.

Work hours will be from 8:30 a.m. to 3:30 p.m., Monday through Friday. Motorists are advised to expect lane closures and delays, and to allow extra travel time while driving through work areas. Concrete work began this week.

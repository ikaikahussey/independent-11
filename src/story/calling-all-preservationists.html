Anyone interested in forming a community-based preservation organization for Kaimuki is invited to an organizational meeting on May 18 at 5:30 p.m..  The location is Kapiolani Community College in room Ohia 118, same as the initial meeting last month.  Topics to be discussed will include nominating leaders, setting objectives, prioritizing the organization’s activities, and general brainstorming.  In addition, updates on the Queen Theater and the KCC / Fort Ruger Chapel will be addressed.  If you are interested in joining the group, but cannot make it to the meeting, please send your contact information to Candace Strong at CandaceS@cbpacific.com to be added to the announcements list.  For questions about the meeting or the organization in general, contact Katie Kastner at Historic Hawaii Foundation at 523-2900x22 or katie@historichawaii.org.

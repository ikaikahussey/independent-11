HONOLULU—Due to the biennial Rim of the Pacific (RIMPAC) maritime exercise, the City and County of Honolulu’s Department of Parks and Recreation will not be issuing camping permits for the weekends of July 9 to 12 and July 23 to 26 for Bellows Field Beach Park.

The City will be issuing permits for camping at Bellows for the weekends of July 2 to 5, July 16 to 19 and July 30 to Aug 1.

RIMPAC 2010 is themed “Combined Agility, Synergy and Support,” and marks the 22nd exercise in the series that originated in 1971.

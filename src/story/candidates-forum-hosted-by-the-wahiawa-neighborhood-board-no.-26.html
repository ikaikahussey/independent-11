A candidates forum hosted by the Wahiawa Neighborhood Board No. 26 happens at Wahiawa Park’s Hale Koa Recreation Center, 1139 Kilani Ave. on Monday, August 16. The forum will take place after the Board’s regularly scheduled meeting which begins at 7:00 p.m. and will be limited to only reports and concerns to accommodate the forum during the same night.

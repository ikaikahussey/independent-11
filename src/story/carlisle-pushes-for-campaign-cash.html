Dave Shapiro reports that Carlisle is starting to solicit early for his next campaign, with a $8,000 per table Mayoral Celebration on April 19 at the Hilton Hawaiian Village.

It’s a new day from his time as city prosecutor when he made a big deal of placing limits on the campaign contributions he’d accept.

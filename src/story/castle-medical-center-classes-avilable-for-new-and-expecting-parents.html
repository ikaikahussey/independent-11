The following is Castle Medical Center’s calendar of upcoming classes for new and expecting parents.

Birth Center Tours
Tuesdays, August 3 and 17 from 5:00 p.m. to 6:00 p.m.
Castle Medical Center, Kailua
Cost: Free
Call (808) 263-5400 or register online at castlemed.org.

General Newborn Care
In this class, learn how to tell if your baby is sick, how to take your baby’s temperature, why babies cry, how to calm a crying baby, and how to bathe your baby.
Tuesday, August 3 from 6:30 p.m. to 9:30 p.m.
Castle Medical Center, Kailua
Cost: $25 for two people
Call (808) 263-5400 or register online at castlemed.org.

Breastfeeding Classes
Thursday, August 12 or Tuesday, August 31 from 6:00 p.m. to 8:30 p.m.
Castle Medical Center, Kailua
Cost: $25. Free with any Castle childbirth class.
Call (808) 263-5400 or register online at castlemed.org.

Lamaze™  Prepared Childbirth
This 6-week course reviews body conditioning exercises, breathing and relaxation techniques. Instructors will also review topics as needed by participants. These may include labor and delivery, postpartum care, medications, or breast and bottle feeding.
6 sessions, Sundays, starting August 22 from 11:00 a.m. to 2:00 p.m.
Castle Medical Center, Kailua
Cost: $75, or $65 if delivering at Castle. Price includes Breast-Feeding class.
Call (808) 263-5400 or register online at castlemed.org.

Infant CPR and Safety
Recommended for parents, grandparents and other non-professional childcare providers, this course teaches basic life support techniques for infants and toddlers as well as how to make your home safe for young children.
Tuesday, Aug. 17 from 6:30 p.m. to 9:30 p.m.
Castle Medical Center, Kailua
Cost: $15
Call (808) 263-5400 or register online at castlemed.org.

The Kaimuki Library is proud to present Award winning local writer Chris McKinney Sunday, May 23 at 2:00 p.m.; admission is free.  He will be discussing writing in general and his own books in particular, which include The Tattoo, Queen of Tears, Bolohead Row, and most recently, Mililani Mauka. 

McKinney is well-renowned and much acclaimed for his depictions of the dark and seamy side of life in contemporary Hawaii.  He is also an instructor of composition and literature at Honolulu Community College.  His writing has received various awards, such as the Elliot Cades Award for Literature and Ka Palapala Pookela Awards for Excellence in Literature and Excellence in Writing Literature. 

For more information, or to arrange for a sign language interpreter, please call the Kaimuki Library at (808) 733-8422.

HONOLULU—The City and County of Honolulu will be offering express TheBus service to the public going to the Pro Bowl this Sunday, January 30 at Aloha Stadium. 

Football fans can relax and avoid the traffic and parking battles to focus on cheering for their teams.

There will be 17 express locations island-wide. The fare is $6.25 each way (cash only, exact fare, no passes, or transfers).

The locations are:

Ala Moana Center
Waikiki
Downtown
Hawaii Kai
Kahala
Kaimuki
University of Hawaii
Palolo
Village Park
Kapolei
Mililani Mauka
Ewa Beach
Kaneohe Bay/Makalani
Kailua: Keolu/Hele
Kailua / Hahani
Kaneohe Bay/Mokapu
Kalanianaole/Kailua

Return Trip: TheBus will leave the stadium when the bus is full and the last return trip will leave a half-hour after the game ends. 

For more schedule information including departure times from the neighborhood locations, call 848-5555 or visit www.thebus.org.

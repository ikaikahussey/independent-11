I’m pleased to announce a new print piece which we’re debuting in coffee shops and stores around the island: “Growing Pains,” a graphical take on population growth in Hawaii. The piece is designed by Joshua Lake of Airspace Workshop, a local design firm. It’s part of our effort both to spread the word about the Independent, and also to find new and innovative ways to tell Hawaii’s story. Hawaii’s explosive growth since 1950 has indeed led to growing pains – a strained water supply, clogged roads, food insecurity, and houselessness. Thanks to Josh for providing a visual starting point for these discussions.

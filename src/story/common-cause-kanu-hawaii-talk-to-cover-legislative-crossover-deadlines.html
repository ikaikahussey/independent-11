Common Cause and Kanu Hawaii meet every Thursday at 5:30 p.m. at the State Capitol Rotunda to discuss what is happening in the legislative calendar, provide tips for how citizens can participate effectively, share updates on priority bills, and visit legislators’ offices. Guest speakers from partner organizations will also share their issues for the legislative session.

The Rotunda Roundups are designed to help everyone get involved at a more convenient time.

Today, March 10, they will be talking about the “crossover” legislative deadline and then visit the Public Access Room on the fourth floor of the Capitol.

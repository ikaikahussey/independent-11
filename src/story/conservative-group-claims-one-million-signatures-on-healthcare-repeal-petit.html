A conservative group critical of the Democrats’ healthcare reform law has collected 1 million signatures calling for the law’s repeal, the group announced Thursday. Revere America, a Washington-based conservative group named after the Revolution-era hero, has posted its petition online, calling for “the repeal and replacement of this law with responsible reforms.” The Hill reports.

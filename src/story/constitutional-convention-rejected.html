With all precincts reporting, voters have rejected a proposed constitutional convention by a margin of 2 to 1, with only 146,021 voters supporting a convention and 269,567 opposed.

Only 4.6% of the voters, or 19,880, left the question blank. According to election rules, these blank votes will be counted as "no" votes.

The Hawaii Independent deeply regrets that certain quotes in the July 1, 2009 story “Lanikai residents seek to restrict beach wedding photography” by freelance writer Sean Aronson were fabricated and wrongly attributed to former Kailua neighborhood board member Kalana Best. Mr. Aronson did not interview Ms. Best. All stories written by Mr. Aronson for The Hawaii Independent have been suspended for review. The Hawaii Independent apologizes for the misinformation and the breach of trust with its readers that this incident has caused.

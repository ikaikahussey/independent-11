CrimeStoppers and the Honolulu Police Department are seeking the public’s assistance in locating a male who is wanted for burglary. On June 18, at approximately 12:30 a.m., a male was observed on video surveillance entering the Kokua Market located at 2462 South King St. via the skylight.  The suspect set off the alarm and fled through the fire exit door.

Suspect: Unknown male
Race: Caucasian
Age: 20 to 30 years old
Clothing: Red/black baseball cap, dark colored hooded sweater, gray shorts, orange backpack, and shoes.

Visit the Crimestoppers website at http://www.crimestoppers-honolulu.org/unsolved.aspx for more details if you have any information about this crime or the person involved or you can call CrimeStoppers Honolulu at (808) 955-8300, dial *crime on your cellular phone (free cellular calls are provided by Cingular, Nextel, T-Mobile, and Verizon Wireless in Hawaii). You do not have to give your name. CrimeStoppers Honolulu will pay a cash reward of up to $1,000 if your tip leads to an arrest, identity of a suspect, or recovery of property, and you can remain anonymous.

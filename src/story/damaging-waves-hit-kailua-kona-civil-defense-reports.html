Via KHON:

UPDATE 5:30 a.m.

The Big Island Civil Defense says damaging tsunami waves have impacted Kailua-Kona.

All Hawaii County offices and facilities will be closed today.  This includes all Parks and Recreation facilities not used as evacuation centers and all beaches.

Bus services will run on a limited schedule.  Please call #961-8556 for more information.

An evacuation order remains in effect.

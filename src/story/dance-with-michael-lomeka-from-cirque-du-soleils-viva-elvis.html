Diamond Head Theatre is hosting a special workshop on Tuesday, July 27, from 7:00 p.m. to 8:30 p.m. in their rehearsal hall. Michael Lomeka will teach a musical theater class that includes warm-up, across the floor, and combinations. Michael currently performs in Cirque du Soleil’s Viva ELVIS fresh off of their appearance on So You Think You Can Dance. A Juilliard graduate, his professional credits include The King and I on Broadway with Marie Osmond, international and national tours with Mikhail Baryshnikov’s White Oak Dance Project, and Celine Dion’s spectacular A New Day choreographed by Mia Michaels.

The fee is $35 per student (age 13s and up, intermediate level and above). For more information, or to register, call (808) 733-0277 ext. 306.

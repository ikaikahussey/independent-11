The death of a veteran Sea World trainer in Orlando, Fla., Wednesday has spotlighted the campaign of several major animal rights groups to keep marine mammals out of theme parks altogether, The Christian Science Monitor reports. Early reports say that the same orca had been involved in two past previous human deaths.

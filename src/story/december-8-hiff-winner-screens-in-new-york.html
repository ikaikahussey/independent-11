Na Oiwi NYC is cosponsoring a free screening of Noho Hewa—The Wrongful Occupatin of Hawaii, which won the Hawaii International Film Festival’s award for best documentary in 2008. The film screening will include a talk with Native Hawaiian filmmaker Anne Keala Kelly and New York University professor Sally E. Merry.

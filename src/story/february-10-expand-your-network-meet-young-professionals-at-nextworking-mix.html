Get connected “Nextworking” with Hawaii’s business professionals at one of Honolulu’s largest young professional mixers. Presented by HPELive, “Nextworking” is being held on Thursday, February 10 at Pearl Ultra Lounge from 6:00 p.m. to 9:00 p.m. Admission is free and no RSVP is needed.

Enjoy Pearl’s famous happy hour specials, win raffle prizes, and have the opportunity to grow your business connections. Past “Nextworking” events have welcomed more than 300 young business professionals.

The event is sponsored by Ad 2 Honolulu, American Marketing Association, Asian American Journalist Association, AT Marketing, Hawaii Jaycees, Honolulu Social Club, HPELive, Pacific Edge Magazine, Potent Productions, and Young Business Council.

For more information, email Nicole@hawaiipacificentertainment.com, call (808) 237-3671 or Twitter “#nextworking”.

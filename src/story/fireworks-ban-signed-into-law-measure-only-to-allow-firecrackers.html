A City and County of Honolulu bill that bans all consumer fireworks except for firecrackers was signed into law today. The ban will begin this coming January 2, allowing Oahu residents to legally have one final New Year’s celebration with all consumer fireworks except for aerials, which are already illegal. KITV4 News reports.

The New York Times’ Robert Mackey has the latest on the Koran Burning Cancellation. Terry Jones is the Florida pastor whose plan to burn copies of the Koran had attracted worldwide attention. Following a meeting with Imam Muhammad Musri, the head of the Islamic Society of Central Florida, Jones announced on Thursday that he will not burn hundreds of Korans to mark the anniversary of the September 11 attacks.

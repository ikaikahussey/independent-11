Three people were found dead following an early Friday shooting that police suspect was a double murder-suicide. The bodies of 43-year-old Clayborne Conley, 46-year-old Kristine Cass and her 13-year-old daughter, Saundra Cass, were found in a Makiki Street home near Round Top Drive. Police said Conley apparently broke into the home and allegedly shot Cass, her daughter and dog before killing himself. Associated Press reports.

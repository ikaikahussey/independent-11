Change.org reports that enrollment in for-profit colleges has grown from about 365,000 students to almost 1.8 million over the last several years, according to the US Government Accountability Office (GOA). But a new report by the GOA reveals that fraudulent and invasive recruitment practices may be at work behind the scenes of the sector’s rapid growth.

MANOA — All UH Mānoa students with a validated UHM identification card are eligible for admission to UH football and women’s volleyball home games, subject to availability of seats – and free bus transportation is also available for this Saturday’s game at Aloha Stadium against the University of California, Davis.

Starting this season, a limited amount of reserved seats for students are available for football games.  For additional details on how to obtain these seats as well as admission to the general student section, visit:  www.hawaiiathletics.com/sports. 

For the UC Davis game, buses will depart from Dole Street in front of Frear Hall and Gateway House promptly at 3 p.m., with the last bus departing at 3:15 p.m.  All buses will return to campus 30 minutes after the conclusion of the game. Seat
availability will be on a first-come, first-served basis. 

Students may enter Aloha Stadium through Gates 1, 7, and 8 and Stan Sheriff Center through Gate A.

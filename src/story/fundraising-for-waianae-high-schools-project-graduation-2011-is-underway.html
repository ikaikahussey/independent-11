Fundraising for Waianae High School’s “Project Graduation 2011” is underway. The goal this year is to raise $45,000 to provide the 2011 graduates a safe night of festivities and transportation after tossing up their mortarboards.

To inquire about upcoming fundraising events, how to “sponsor a senior,” or how to donate to Waianae High School Project Grad, call (808) 620-7604 or (808) 520-4542.

County of Hawaii

Hawaii County Clerk’s Office—Hamakua Conference Room
25 Aupuni Street
Hilo, HI 96720
Monday to Saturday: 8:00 a.m. to 4:00 p.m.

Kona Elections Satellite Office—Alii Plaza building
75-143 Hualalai Road, #102
Kailua-Kona, HI 96740
Monday to Saturday: 8:00 a.m. to 4:00 p.m.

The following County of Hawaii locations will be open till October 29:

Waimea Community Center

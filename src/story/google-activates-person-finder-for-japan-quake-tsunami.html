SFGate.com blogged that Google Inc. activated a Person Finder page to help people concerned about loved ones in the area affected by Friday’s devastating earthquake and tsunami in Japan.

The Person Finder page can be found at http://japan.person-finder.appspot.com and it is available both in English and Japanese.

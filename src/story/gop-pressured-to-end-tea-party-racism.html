A Latino advocacy group has launched an online campaign pressuring top Republican leaders to apologize for “inciting Tea Party racism,”  blogs NPR’s Lee Hill. The campaign comes after incidences of vandalism, spitting, and racial and homophobic remarks were allegedly targeted at members of Congress this week.

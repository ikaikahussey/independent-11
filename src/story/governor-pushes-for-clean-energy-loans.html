In his State of the State address, Governor Abercrombie described an idea which was mentioned at the Ideas Summit: on-bill financing. The Governor said:

By utilizing moneys deposited in a special fund to provide capitalization, we will provide a periodic payment system that can be assessed through a customerâ€™s electric utility bill that will amortize the costs of the system over a sustained period, allowing more of us to take advantage of renewable energy in our homes, our businesses, our churches and our communities.  No longer will a photovoltaic system be only available to those who can afford the significant initial investment.

WAIKIKI—Greenroom Gallery is proud to present its first major gallery exhibition, Town, which will be on display from August 20 to September 19. Major contributing artists to this contemporary surf art show will include Tyler Warren, Andy Davis, and Jeff Canham; it will be the first Hawaii venue for all three artists.

An opening reception party is planned for Friday, August 20,  from 6:00 p.m. to 9:00 p.m., where guests can enjoy light refreshments and pupus. All three artists will be in attendance for meet and greets, followed by an autograph signing.

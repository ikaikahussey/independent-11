The massive yearlong “H-1 Rehabilitation” project got underway Sunday with a complete shutdown of the highway’s eastbound lanes from Likelike Highway to Ward Avenue.

After months of advance notice to the public, work has begun on a year-long project to resurface and make other improvements to a busy stretch of the H-1 Freeway.

HONOLULU—Congresswoman Colleen Hanabusa will hold a series of town hall meetings in Honolulu while the U.S. House is in Spring recess, beginning on Wednesday, April 20 at Moanalua Elementary School.

Hanabusa serves on both the House Armed Services and Natural Resources Committees. She most recently voted with fellow House Democrats Wednesday to repeal the Prevention and Public Health Fund Act amid partisan wrangling over the federal budget.

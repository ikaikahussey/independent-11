Democrat Mufi Hannemann is raising more money than his rivals in his run for Hawaii governor, outperforming Republican James “Duke” Aiona and Democrat Neil Abercrombie. Hannemann’s campaign reported Monday it raised a total of nearly $2.5 million through June 30, compared to $2.4 million for Aiona and $2.1 million for Abercrombie. Associated Press reports.

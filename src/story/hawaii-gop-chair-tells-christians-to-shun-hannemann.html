KITV4’s Denby Fawcett reports on a letter being circulated written by Hawaii State GOP Chairman Jonah Kaauwai, which warns conservative Christians in Hawaii that “[Gubernatorial candidate Mufi] Hannemann’s campaign strategy is a trap of lies and baited deception and is being fueled by that very fear. Do not be deceived.” Republican candidate Duke Aiona denounced the letter, saying it was divisive.

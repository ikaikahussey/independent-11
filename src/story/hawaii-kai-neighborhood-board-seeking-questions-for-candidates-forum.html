HAWAII KAI—The Hawaii Kai Neighborhood Board is holding a candidates forum on Tuesday, September 28, for candidates in Senate Districts 8 and 25, House District 18, and all Oahu Board of Education races.

After a brief business meeting at 7:00 p.m., the legislative races forum will run from 7:30 to 8:30 p.m., followed by the BOE forum from 8:30 to 9:30 p.m. Candidates and the public are welcome between 6:30 p.m. and 10:00 p.m. to meet and talk before and after the forum at Hahaione Elementary School, 595 Pepeekeo Street.

Coming after the September 18 primary, the forum will include only those candidates that are advancing to the November 2 general election.

Beth-Ann Kozlovich, Hawaii Public Radio’s executive producer of talk shows and host of “Town Square,” will serve as moderator.

The Board is asking the public to submit questions for the candidates in advance. Questions may be sent to knudsen123@gmail.com by September 24.

The meeting and candidates forum will be cablecast on Olelo Channel 54 each Tuesday in October at 9 p.m. Additional cablecasts are possible.

The Board’s August 31 forum with mayoral and City Council District 4 candidates will be cablecast on Olelo Channel 54 on Tuesday, September 14, at 9:00 p.m., and can be viewed any time at http://Makakilo.com/HawaiiKaiNB.html.

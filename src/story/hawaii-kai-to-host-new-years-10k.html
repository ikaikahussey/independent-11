HAWAII KAI—The Bosetti 10K Run, sponsored by the Mid-Pacific Road Runners Club, is set for Saturday, January 1, 2011, at 7:00 a.m.

The race will start at Kealahou Street near Sandy Beach, then head down Hawaii Kai Drive and Lunalilo Home Road and turn around on Kolokolo Street, and back to Lunalilo Home Road and Hawaii Kai Drive to end at Kealahou Street. Approximately 100 runners are expected to participate. 

For more information, contact John Simmonds at (808) 373-3609 or email simondsj001@hawaii.rr.com.

A Hawaii state lawmaker intends to introduce a symbolic resolution banning the N-word after he heard talk radio host Dr. Laura Schlessinger say it on air. Democratic Rep. John Mizuno said Friday the resolution wouldn’t have the effect of law, but he hopes it would send a message that the word is hateful and offensive. Associated Press reports.

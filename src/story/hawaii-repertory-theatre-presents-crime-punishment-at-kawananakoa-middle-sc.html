The Hawaii Repertory Theater will premiere in Hawaii a dramatic version of Dostoeyevsky’s Crime and Punishment on Friday, October 8 at 7:30 p.m. at the Backstage Theater at Kawananakoa Middle School.

Adapted by Marilyn Campbell and Curt Columbus, the three-person, 90-minute play has received critical acclaim across the United States.

For show times and more information, visit hawaiireptheatre.org.

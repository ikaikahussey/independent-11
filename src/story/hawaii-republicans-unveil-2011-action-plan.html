Hawaii Republicans gathered in a show of unity and to unveil a legislative action plan outlining their proposals to improve Hawaii’s economy, education and government. KITV4 News reports. One item in the action plan calls for changing the House rules to require a super majority to raise taxes or introduce fees, charges, and surcharges. The plan also calls for an independent audit of the Department of Education and an appointed board of education.

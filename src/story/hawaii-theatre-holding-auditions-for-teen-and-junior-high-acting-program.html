The Hawaii Theatre Center is holding audtions in July and August for its teen and junior high acting program and theatre apprentices for the 2010-2011 season.

Hawaii Theatre Center will hold its second round of group auditions on Saturday, July 17, for its 2010-2011 student programs, which take place after school from September through May. The last date for group auditions will be on Saturday, August 7. No experience is necessary.

Star-Bulletin Publisher Dennis Francis said yesterday the new Star-Advertiser will employ about 450 workers, a cut of about 430 Honolulu Advertiser jobs.

Hawaii blogger Ian Lind posted a complete list of the few Honolulu Advertiser staff who will be joining the new newspaper.

The Hawaii Community Development Authority, a quasi-public entity charged with developing state lands, is holding two supplemental public comment sessions regarding three highrise developments in Kakaako – 803 Waimanu, 404 Ward Avenue, and 604 Ala Moana Blvd.

The meetings are scheduled for Saturday, June 22, 2013 at 10 am, and Monday, June 24, 2013 at 5:30 pm, at the HCDA offices, at 461 Cooke Street.

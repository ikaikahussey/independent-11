State Sen. Fred Hemmings, who represents the 25th District (from Lanikai to Waimanalo), says that his wife Lydia Hemmings was wrongfully charged with stealing from a nonprofit organization she had led. Sen. Hemmings attributes charges brought by the Attorney General’s office to his role as a State senator. The Star Advertiser reports.

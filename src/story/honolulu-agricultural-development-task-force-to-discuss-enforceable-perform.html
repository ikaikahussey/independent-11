HONOLULU—The City and County of Honolulu’s Agricultural Development Task Force will be meeting Monday, November 1 in the City Council Meeting Room, 2nd floor Honolulu Hale, at 1:00 p.m.

On the agenda is developing a working definition of agriculture, which includes enforceable standards and performance standards. The task force will also hear new member recommendations to fill vacancies.

The Honolulu Agricultural Development Task Force was established to make recommendations to enhance the viability of the agricultural industry on Oahu.

The next scheduled meeting is for Monday, December 6 at 1:00 p.m. The Agricultural Development Task Force generally meets on the first Monday of each month, unless there is a holiday

For more information, email task force chair Yukio Kitagawa at cclbc@honolulu.gov.

HONOLULU—The City and County of Honolulu will hold a special forum on houselessness on Tuesday, June 29 from 4:30 p.m. to 7:00 p.m. at the Mission Memorial Auditorium (530 South King St., beside Honolulu Hale). The public is invited.

The forum will include a comprehensive overview of the complexities, challenges, and services available to help address houselessness on Oahu. The Cityâ€™s latest homelessness “Point in Time Count Report” will also be released. Various public officials, service providers, civic organizations and volunteers are expected to attend.

Attendance is free, but RSVPs are encouraged by noon on Monday, June 28.

Free parking will be available in the Fasi Municipal Building parking lot (1100 Alapai St.).

For more information, the public may contact Debbie Miyagi of the Department of Community Services at (808) 768-7760 or dmiyagi@honolulu.gov.

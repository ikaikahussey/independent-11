KITV4 News reports that rail, not surprisingly, took center state at Tuesday’s Honolulu mayoral debate:

“You were asked by Mahea Richardson whether or not rail can be stopped, to which you responded it can be stopped. Executive privilege has its privileges,” former Honolulu Prosecutor Peter Carlisle directed at Prevedouros.

Prevedouros responded with, “The amendment we passed says shall, well I may shall do rail, but for now I don’t shall have any money because they money was shall taken for the sewers.”

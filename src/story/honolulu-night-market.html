On June 15th, the Honolulu Night Market took place in the heart of Kakaako. The main attraction of the night were the Red Bull athletes: Keith Sayers, Wes Agee, and Ronnie Renner http://www.redbullxfighters.com. All three performed high flying action that sparked a lot of oooohs and aaaaaahs. Other attractions include food stands, art trucks, a skateboard ramp, and lastly a fashion show premiered by Barrio Vintage.

The Honolulu Night Market is a monthly event held every third Saturday. For more information, visit their website: www.honolulunightmarket.com

For District 3 Sector 3 which includes Aiea and Pearl Kai a total of 32 calls were made regarding incidents of theft to the HPD from 4/7/2010 through 4/20/2010. There were also 20 reports of unauthorized entry of a motor vehicle theft.

For District 3 Sector 2 which includes Pearl City and Pacific Palisades a total of 27 calls were made regarding incidents of theft and 36 incidents of unauthorized entry of a motor vehicle.

The initial classifications are subject to change.

For full HPD report visit http://www.honolulupd.org/statistics/index.htm.

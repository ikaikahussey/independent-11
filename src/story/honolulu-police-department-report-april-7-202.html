For District 8 Sector 3 which includes Kapolei, Ewa and Kalaeloa, a total of 29 calls were made regarding incidents of theft to the HPD from 4/7/2010 through 4/20/2010. There were also 20 reports of burglary.

The initial classifications are subject to change.

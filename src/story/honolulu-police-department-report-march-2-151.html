For District 8 Sector 3 which includes Kapolei, Ewa and Kalaeloa, a total of 38 calls were made regarding incidents of theft to the HPD from 3/2/2010 through 3/15/2010. There were also 16 reports of unauthorized entry of a motor vehicle.

The initial classifications are subject to change.

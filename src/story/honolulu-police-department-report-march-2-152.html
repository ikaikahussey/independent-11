For District 3 Sector 3 which includes Aiea and Pearl Kai a total of 29 calls were made regarding incidents of theft to the HPD from 3/2/2010 through 3/15/2010. There were also 25 reports of unauthorized entry of a motor vehicle theft.

For District 3 Sector 2 which includes Pearl City and Pacific Palisades a total of 40 calls were made regarding incidents of unauthorized entry of a motor vehicle and 27 incidents of theft.

The initial classifications are subject to change.

For full HPD report visit http://www.honolulupd.org/statistics/index.htm.

For Honolulu’s District 8, Sector 3 which includes Kapolei, Ewa, and Kalaeloa, a total of 28 calls were made regarding incidents of theft to the Honolulu Police Department from September 23 through October 3. There were also 8 reports of burglary.

The initial classifications are subject to change.

For the full HPD report, visit http://www.honolulupd.org/statistics/weekly/d8_sector_3_rpt.pdf.

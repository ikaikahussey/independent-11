Yes, we would prefer you buy gifts locally when possible, but for those of you who still have gifts to buy for your family or friends in the Continental United States should take advantage of National Free Shipping Day on Friday, December 17. Free shipping is only available within the Continental U.S.  To find a list of retailers participating visit http://www.freeshippingday.com.

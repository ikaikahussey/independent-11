Honolulu Magazine notes that there are only 61 daily news reporters in Honolulu in 2010, compared to 86 in 2005. These numbers don’t count the dozens of broadcast reporters who lost their jobs in the TV station merger, or reporters who wrote for smaller weekly or monthly publications that have since shrunk or folded.

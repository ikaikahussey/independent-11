The tax-by-postcode is even more apparent in the Unites States where states’ revenue-raising powers produce some very large disparities. State income taxes in the U.S. vary from nothing in Wyoming, Texas, Nevada, Florida, and South Dakota to peak income rates of 11 percent in Hawaii and 9 percent in New Jersey.

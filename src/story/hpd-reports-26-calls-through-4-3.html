A total of 26 calls were made to the Honolulu Police Department from 3/21/2010 through 4/3/2010 regarding auto theft recovery, motor vehicle theft, unauthorized entry into a motor vehicle, burglary, theft, and graffiti type incidents for Waialua and the North Shore.

The initial classifications are subject to change.

For more information please visit http://www.honolulupd.org/statistics/index.htm.

Auto theft, burglary, and graffiti reported for Waipio, Mililani, and Mililani Mauka.

A total of 41 calls were made to the Honolulu Police Department from 4/07/2010 through 4/20/2010 regarding auto theft recovery, motor vehicle theft, unauthorized entry into a motor vehicle, burglary, theft, and graffiti type incidents for Waipio, Mililani, and Mililani Mauka.

A total of 61 calls were made to the Honolulu Police Department from 3/7/2010 through 3/20/2010 regarding auto theft recovery, motor vehicle theft, unauthorized entry into a motor vehicle, burglary, theft, and graffiti type incidents for Waipio, Mililani, and Mililani Mauka

The initial classifications are subject to change.

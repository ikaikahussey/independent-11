A total of 62 calls were made to the Honolulu Police Department from 2/27/2010 through 3/12/2010 regarding auto theft recovery, motor vehicle theft, unauthorized entry into a motor vehicle, burglary, theft, and graffiti type incidents for Waipio, Mililani, and Mililani Mauka

The initial classifications are subject to change.

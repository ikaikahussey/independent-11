President Barack Obama, in a striking legal and political shift, has determined that the Defense of Marriage Act—the 1996 law that bars federal recognition of same-sex marriages—is unconstitutional, and has directed the Justice Department to stop defending the law in court, the administration said Wednesday. The New York Times reports.

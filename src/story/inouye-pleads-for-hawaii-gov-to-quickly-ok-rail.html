U.S. Sen. Daniel Inouye is stepping up to bat for rail. On Wednesday, Inouye “strongly urged” Gov. Linda Lingle to approve a plan to build a mass transit rail line in Honolulu, saying federal funds for project could be in jeopardy if she doesn’t do so quickly. Associated Press reports.

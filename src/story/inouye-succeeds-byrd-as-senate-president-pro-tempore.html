The Washington Post’s Paul Kane blogs that Sen. Daniel Inouye (D-Hawaii), the most senior member of the Senate, will be sworn in as president pro-tempore of the Senate, placing him third in the line of succession to become president, following the death of Robert C. Byrd (D-W.Va.).

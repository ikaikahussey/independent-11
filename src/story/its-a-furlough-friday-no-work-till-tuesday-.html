HONOLULU—Most City and County of Honolulu government offices will be closed on Friday, September 3, due to an employee furlough day and Monday, September 6, in observance of Labor Day, a federal holiday.

Here’s the skinny on this weekend’s services:

* Emergency services will be available.
* Regular refuse collections will be made.   
* Municipal golf courses and the Honolulu Zoo remain open.
* Honolulu and Kapolei Hale will open for absentee voting Friday, September 3 to Thursday, September 16 from 8:00 a.m. to 4:00 p.m. The absentee polling place is open on Furlough Friday but closed on Labor Day and Sundays.
* Refuse and recycling convenience centers will be closed on Furlough Friday and open on Labor Day.
* Indoor recreation centers and botanical gardens will be closed for the Furlough and open on Labor Day.
* People’s Open Markets will not be held.
* All Satellite City Halls and driver license offices will be closed.
* Buses will operate on a regular schedule for Furlough Friday and a Sunday schedule for Labor Day. TheHandi-Van service will operate as usual.

The following traffic and parking regulations will be in effect for Labor Day:

路    On-street parking will be free, except for the meters on Kalakaua Avenue along Queen Kapiolani Park.
路    Parking will be permitted in tow-away zones that allow parking on holidays.
路    There will be no coning of traffic lanes.
路    Traffic signals at Kalakaua Avenue and Kapiolani Boulevard will be three-phase to permit mauka-bound traffic to turn left onto Kapiolani Boulevard.

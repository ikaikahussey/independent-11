A celebration of civil rights happens this Saturday as part of mini lecture series aimed at gathering support to educate Waianae’s children and community. “Celebrate the Civil Rights of All” takes place at Leeward Community College Waianae to honor our civil rights and Dr. Martin Luther King, Jr.

Join the call to unity in education, employment, and justice and celebrate the diversity of the Leeward Coast.

“Celebrate the Civil Rights of All”
Saturday, January 8 from 4:30 p.m. to 8:00 p.m.
Leeward Community College Waianae
86-088 Farrington Hwy, Room #101

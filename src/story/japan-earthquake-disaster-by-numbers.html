The Telegraph reports on the latest official numbers of the 2011 Sendai earthquake and tsunami as of Wednesday March 16.

4,164 –- The latest death official toll.
11,000 –- The number of people who are officially missing in the earthquake and tsunami.
3.5 trillion yen –- Pumped into the Japanese financial system to prevent market collapse.
452,000 –- People housed in temporary shelters
180 –- Workers at the Fukushima nuclear plant being rotated in an out of the danger zone to minimise their exposure.

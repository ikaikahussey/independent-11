The Little League team from Japan beat the 2010 U.S. championship team from Waipahu, Hawaii to take the Little League World Series title with a 4-1 victory Sunday. Hawaii, which had scored 29 runs over its previous three games, came up short at the plate against Tokyo. It was Hawaii’s fifth game in five days. Associated Press reports.

The Libyan government freed a detained Al-Jazeera journalist from custody today, the second reporter released by the regime of Muammar Gaddafi this week, Global Post reports. Meanwhile, Libyan authorities have still not yet confirmed the whereabouts of four Western journalists — including a correspondent for GlobalPost — who went missing in eastern Libya earlier this month.

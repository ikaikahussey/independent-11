An LGBT and Allies Equality Potluck takes place on Saturday, July 10 from 12:00 p.m. to 4:00 p.m. at Ala Moana Beach Park - Picnic Area 3.

Various organizations are sponsoring the event in order to come together and talk, share stories, and look toward the future. Sponsors include PFLAG-Oahu, Pride Alliance Hawaii, Equality Hawaii, GLBT Caucus of the Democratic Party of Hawaii, Honolulu LGBT Pride Committee, HRC, and COLAGE.

The event is a potluck. RSVP here and list what you are planning on bringing to share.

The potluck is free and open to the public. Look for the rainbow flags.

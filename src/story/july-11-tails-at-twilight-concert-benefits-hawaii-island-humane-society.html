“Tails at Twilight: A Concert for Animals” on Hawaii Island features music all about animals, with performances of favorite musical theater and opera works by the artists of the Hawaii Performing Arts Festival. The concert happens Sunday, July 11 from 4:00 p.m. at the Four Seasons Resort Hualalai ballroom.

The concert benefits Hawaii Island Humane Society and the Hawaii Performing Arts Festival. Tickets are $60 per person and includes a wonderful afternoon of music. Tickets are available at HIHS shelters in Kona, Waimea, and Keaau or by calling (808) 329-2135 ext. 3.

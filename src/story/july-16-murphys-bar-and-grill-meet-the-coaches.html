The newest University of Hawai?i coaches for men’s volleyball and women’s basketball ring in their welcome at Murphy’s Bar and Grill in Downtown.

Charlie Wade, the new men’s volleyball head coach, and Dana Takahara-Dias, the new women’s basketball head coach will be on hand enjoying music and pupus with UH fans.

Wade, the program?s fifth head coach, is a former UH women?s volleyball assistant from 1995-2005, and was most recently the head women?s volleyball coach at University of the Pacific. Joining Wade?s staff is associate coach Dan Fisher and assistant coach Mason Kuo.

Takahara-Dias, a four-year UH letterwinner from 1984-88, is the program?s seventh head coach since becoming a varsity sport in 1974 and first woman head coach since Patsy Dung (1974-79). Joining Takahara-Dias? staff is associate coach Da Houl, assistant coach Serenda Valdez, and director of operations Sherice Ajifu.

Thursday, July 16, from 5:30 p.m. until 9:00 p.m. at Murphy’s Bar and Grill. Merchant Street will be closed and there will be live music by Firehouse, food, and beverages. Admission is free. For more information, call 531-0422.

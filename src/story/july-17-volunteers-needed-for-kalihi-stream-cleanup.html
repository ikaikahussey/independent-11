Volunteers are needed for a Kalihi Stream cleanup that will occur on Saturday, July 17 from 8:30 a.m. to 11:30 a.m. on the grounds of the Kuhio Park Terrace Housing.

Enter through the School Street guard house, turn left into the driveway to a parking lot. Park near the green dumpster located on the left side of the driveway.

For more information, contact (808) 372-8365 or louise.cayetano@gmail.com.

The 40th Annual Roy Sakuma Ukulele Festival Hawaii will take place on Sunday, July 18 between 9:30 a.m. and 1:30 p.m. in the Kapiolani Park Bandstand on the Diamond Head end of Waikiki.

Ukulele virtuosos, internationally known musicians, local celebrities donate their time as they join talented ukulele players from around the world and an ukulele orchestra of over 800 students.

Festivities include free music, food booths, ukulele displays, and an ukulele giveaway.

For more information visit the official Ukulele Festival Hawaii website.

40th Annual Ukulele Festival
Sunday, July 18 from 9:30 a.m. to 2:30 p.m.
Kapiolani Park Bandstand in Waikiki

Shoppers and music lovers alike are invited to the first ART&FLEA, hip new market at Fresh Café.

ART&FLEA, is an urban inspired, one-stop shop to buy and sell locally that will feature vintage collectors, clothing designers, DIYers, independent artists, and handmade aficionados. This month will highlight artists Jeremy Strength, Erik Patton, Grady Gillan, and John Hook with local designers Erica Miller, Michelle Kowalsky, Ashley Ishii, Reva Canali, and more.

Immediately following the market, ART&FLEA will host drink specials and showcase a jam packed lineup of emerging local music acts. This month will feature Uvovu+Yeda , Shopping List , Persian Excursion, Narwhal, and DJ CAPECOD.

New Vendors, musicians, and interested sponsors are welcome. Send inquiries to artandflea@gmail.com or call (808) 222-4118 or (808) 341-4455.

ART&FLEA
Fresh Café, 831 Queen St.
Thursday, July 22
5:00 p.m. to 9:00 p.m.—Art & Flea Market
9:00 p.m. to 2:00 a.m.—After Party, featuring live music
Admission is free before 9:00 p.m., $6 after 9:00 p.m.
All ages welcome

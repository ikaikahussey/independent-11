More than 20 boats will join Lanakila Kitchen to “fish for meals” this Saturday to see who can bring in the biggest Papio catch in the Second Annual Papio Tournament. Winning teams will receive up to $5,000 in cash prizes. All proceeds benefit Lanakila Meals on Wheels, Hawaii’s largest and only island-wide meal provider for seniors.

Boats launch from Heeia Pier in Kaneohe Bay. A team may consist of any number of participants, including the captain. Youths must be registered on application for the The Keiki Division (15 years and under).

There’s limited space, so book early to guarantee your spot. Applications must be completed and tournament fees paid in full by Friday, July 23.

For more information, visit xpresschefs.com.

Lanakila Kitchen’s Second Annual Papio Tournament
Saturday, July 24 from 9:00 a.m. to 12:00 p.m.,
Weighing/judging/prizes through 1:00 p.m.
Heeia Pier in Kaneohe Bay
46-499 Kamehameha Hwy.

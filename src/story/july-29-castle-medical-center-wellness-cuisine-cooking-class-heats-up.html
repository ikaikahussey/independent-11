“Colorful Creations” is the theme of Castle Medical Center’s Wellness Cuisine cooking class, which will be held on Thursday, July 29 from 11:00 a.m. to 1:00 p.m. in the Wellness Center Auditorium at Castle Medical Center.

Let nature’s palette of colors be an inspiration as you learn to use the garden’s bounty to paint an edible masterpiece on your plate. Castle Medical Center’s Wellness educator Eileen Towata uses a variety of raw and cooked plant foods as she shows you how to prepare eye-pleasing and taste bud–pleasing dishes for you and your family.

Cost is $15 per person; $20 per couple; or $10 for seniors and students (with ID). Class seating is limited, and payment and reservations are required two days in advance. For more information, call (808) 263-5400 or register online at castlemed.org.

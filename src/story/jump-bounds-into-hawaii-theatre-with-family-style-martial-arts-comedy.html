The Korean production JUMP! brings a mix of martial arts acrobatics and slapstick comedy to Hawaii Theatre for a performance run from Tuesday, April 5 through Sunday, April 17. The show has sold out houses from Broadway to Hong Kong, thrilling audiences without special effects, wires or a net.

Set in a traditional Korean home, JUMP! plays off of the relationships between a strict patriarch and his three-generational family, which includes a love-struck granddaughter and a drunken uncle. Combining astonishing gymnastics and cheeky slapstick, the show provides wows and laughs for the whole family.

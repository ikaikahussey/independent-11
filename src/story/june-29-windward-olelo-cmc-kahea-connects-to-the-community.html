The staff at Windward Olelo’s community media center (CMC) is inviting the public to their Kahea on Tuesday, June 29 at 6:00 p.m. to celebrate one year at King Intermediate and 5 years in the Windward community.

There will be entertainment, food, updates from the Olelo CEO, and an opportunity to express views with Olelo staff: What services would you like your CMC to offer? What kind of equipment would help you in your creation of programs? Do you need assistance with your current or future programs?

Come and meet your friendly Windward Community Media Center Staff and your fellow community client volunteers and find ways to help each other in the creation of programs that share all of the beauty, resources, struggles, and triumphs that make the Windward community special.

Windward CMC Kahea
Tuesday, June 29 from 6:00 p.m. to 8:00 p.m.
King Intermediate School, Bldg. G, Room 2
46-155 Kamehameha Hwy.

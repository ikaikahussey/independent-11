HONOLULU—Catherine’s Closet Vintage Boutique, 125 Merchant St., is Honolulu’s hot spot for all things vintage. With a hullabaloo of hats, vintage clothes, and gloves, Catherine’s has is a haven for Hawaii’s fashion treasure seekers. The boutique caters to women of all ages and has plans to develop its menswear in the near future.

Catherine’s has been featured in Dysfunction magazine as well as the Honolulu Advertiser, Honolulu Star Bulletin, and Honolulu Magazine, where Catherine’s was chosen as Honolulu’s Best Spot for Vintage Jewelry.

Stop by Catherine’s new location at 125 Merchant Street in downtown Honolulu on Friday, June 4 for a swanky affair, “First Friday: Mad Men Style.” Catherine’s will be open late for First Friday, from 4:00 p.m. to 8:00 p.m., for good music and informal modeling. And, if you’re lucky, a “tini martini!”

First Friday: Mad Men Style
Friday, June 4 from 4:00 p.m. to 8:00 p.m.
Catherine’s Closet Vintage Boutique
125 Merchant St.

Laughtrack Theater on Bethel Street will hold its monthy open mic night on Thursday, January 20. Open to stand-up comedians, poets, musicians, and writers, the Open Mic Jam welcomes all those with the courage to take the stage.

The $5 admission is free for performers. Sign up begins at 7:30 p.m. The organizers encourage all to test new material on a live, supportive, and enthusiastic audience, and even offer reasonable tech support for those performers with specific technical needs.

For reservations call (808) 384-3362.

Open Mic Jam
Thursday, January 20 at 7:30 p.m.
Laughtrack Theater
1123 Bethel Street
Cost $5

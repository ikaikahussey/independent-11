The Hawaii Arts Alliance and the Hawaii Association of Independent Schools are partnering with It’s All About Kids to present the Schools of the Future Conference on Thursday, October 14. The conference features Sir Ken Robinson, an internationally renowned expert on the development of creativity, innovation, and human resources.

Robinson’s work Unlocking Creativity was adopted by business, education and cultural leaders in Northern Ireland during the peace process that followed decades of sectarian violence there. His newest work, The Element: How Finding Your Passion Changes Everything, is a New York Times bestseller.

For registration and program information, visit http://www.hais.org/program_workshops.asp.

Schools of the Future Conference
Thursday, October 14 from 7:30 a.m.
Hilton Hawaiian Village

Honolulu Police Department and Honolulu Fire Department statistics for the month of August were discussed at the September 15 meeting of the Kalihi-Palama Neighborhood Board. As Kalihi-Palama comprises parts of HPD Districts 1 and 5, stats for both were reported. A total of 26 assaults, 5 DUIs, and three sex offenses were among hundreds of HPD responses. HPD noted a rise in motor vehicle thefts and a decrease in motor vehicle collisions. HFD responded to nine fires and 109 medical emergencies.

Also discussed was a proposed addition to the skate facility at Aala Park. A local business came forward with an offer of fundraising support. The City and County of Honolulu has no plans for expansion at the facility, but does support such a project if funding comes from the private sector.

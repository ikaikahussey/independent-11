The ladies of Cherry Blossom Cabaret are reprising their interpretation of the Lewis Carroll classic with Alice in Chinatown at Mark’s Garage this weekend and next. The show played to sold out houses in the spring, and popular demand has called for an encore run. The costuming, soundtrack, and choreography amount to far more than a striptease show, and offer cheeky insight into nightlife in Chinatown.

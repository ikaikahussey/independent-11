The documentary film One Voice will be screened in conjunction with the Hawaii International Film Festival at Sunset on the Beach at Queen’s Beach in Waikiki on Saturday. The film tells the story of the Kamehameha Schools song contest, a tradition of nearly 90 years that fosters leadership and community among the school’s students. The song contest serves an important function in preserving the Hawaiian language, culture and spirit of aloha.


Sunset on the Beach
Saturday, October 23 at 6:45 p.m.
Queen’s Beach, Waikiki

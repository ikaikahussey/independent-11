Honolulu blues gurus Slim Junior will perform at The Venue on Thursday, October 7 at 6:00 p.m. Featuring Rick Smith and Larry Spalding, Slim Junior honors the blues tradition of telling it like it is and playing it like it feels. Smith has hinted at possible guest sit-ins by other accomplished blues players, which makes the free admission even more enticing.

Slim Junior
Thursday, October 7 from 6:00 p.m. to 9:00 p.m.
The Venue
1144 Bethel Street
Free admission

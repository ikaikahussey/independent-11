The improv rock comedy duo Oil in the Alley will be recording all of their October performances for possible inclusion in an album set for release in 2011. They are slated to play two shows at Laughtrack Theater on Bethel Street on Friday, October 29. This gives their growing legion of fans the opportunity to become a part of the art they create, simply by attending the show.

Oil in the Alley will perform at 8:00 p.m. and 10:00 p.m. on that Friday night, with the accomplished improv groups Muslin Hamster and Aftermath also sharing the bill for the evening.

Visit laughtracktheater.com for more information.

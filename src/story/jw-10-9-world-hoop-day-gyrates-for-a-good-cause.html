HulaCoops, a Honolulu-based group of hula hoop enthusiasts are hold an event in honor of World Hoop Day at Kapiolani Park on Sunday, October 10 at 5:00 p.m. The event will also serve as a fundraiser for World Hoop Day, a non-profit organization that benefits underprivileged children around the world.

All of proceeds from a bake sale and all donations will go directly to World Hoop Day. All donations are tax deductible. There will be hula hoop demonstrations, music, and refreshments at the Kapiolani Park event.

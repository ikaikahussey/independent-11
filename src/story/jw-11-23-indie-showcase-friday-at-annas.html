Anna O’Brien’s (formerly Anna Bannana’s) is set to host “Never Say Die” on Friday, November 26. Never Say Die is a showcase of local indie bands, this time featuring The Jumpoffs, Shopping List, Black Sapote, and I Tramonti. The new owners are continuing the 40-year Anna Bannana’s tradition of being a haven and a hotspot for independent, local music.

Never Say Die
Friday, November 26
Anna O’Brien’s
2440 South Beretania Street
Doors at 7:00 p.m.
Free admission before 9:00 p.m., $5 after
21 and over only

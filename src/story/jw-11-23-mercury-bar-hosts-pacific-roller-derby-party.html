Mercury Bar on Chaplain Lane in Chinatown, in collaboration with ladies of Pacific Roller Derby and Black Cat Tattoo, are throwing a roller derby themed party on Wednesday. The evening will feature a screening of the movie “Whip It” and a performance by indie darling Jess Penner. DJ Otto and DJ Fuzz will be cranking out dance hits from the 70s and 80s.

Black Cat Tattoo is sponsoring a dance-off, with a grand prize of a $100 gift certificate for anything at their nearby shop. There will also be an all-female arm wrestling tournament with a $5 entry fee. The winning arm will win the pot.


Pacific Roller Derby and Black Cat Tattoo rally
Wednesday, November 24 at 8:00 p.m.
1154 Fort Street (on Chaplain Lane)

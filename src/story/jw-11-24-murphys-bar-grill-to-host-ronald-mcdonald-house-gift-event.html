Murphy’s Bar and Grill will hold a special block party on Wednesday, December 8 to benefit the Ronald McDonald House of Hawaii. Attendees are encouraged to bring unwrapped gifts for the children of Ronald McDonald House, which will then be wrapped by volunteers. Merchant Street will be closed for the event, and food and drinks will be available on the street.

The Ronald McDonald House of Hawaii assists hundreds of families of seriously ill children from many cultures throughout the Pacific Basin. The organization provides lodging for those families that are in Hawaii as their children undergo treatment at a variety of hospitals.

Ronald McDonald House of Hawaii benefit
Wednesday, December 8 from 6:00 p.m.
Murphy’s Bar & Grill
2 Merchant Street

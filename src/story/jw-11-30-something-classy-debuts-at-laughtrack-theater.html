The newest graduates of the Fundmentals of Improv program at Laughtrack Theater will debut as Something Classy on Saturday, December 4 at 8:00 p.m. and 10:00 p.m.. Adopting the “Harold” form of improvisational comedy, Something Classy will work with an audience suggestion to create a unique, one-time only comedic performance.

Laughtrack Theater Artistic Director Shannon Winpenny is a ten-year veteran of the legendary Secondy City Conservatory in Chicago, and has performed with Honolulu’s own Loose Screws improv group. Laughtrack Theater opened on Bethel Street in Chinatown on September 5, 2008.

For more information, visit laughtracktheater.com.

Longtime local radio personality Phil Roberts is now also a published author with the release of his new book Waikiki Tiki: Art, History and Photographs. There will be a book launch party at Duke’s Canoe Club in Waikiki on Sunday, November 7. The party will be followed by Henry Kapono’s performance on the pool deck. Kapono provides the book’s introduction.

Released by local publishing house Bess Press, Waikiki Tiki traces the colorful history of the tiki phenomenon and the people who helped create the idiom. Bess Press is best known for publishing educational and popular general interest titles from Hawaii and the Pacific. Visit besspress.com for more information about the book and the launch party.

Waikiki Tiki launch party
Sunday, November 7 at 4:00 p.m.
Duke’s Canoe Club
2335 Kalakaua Avenue, #116

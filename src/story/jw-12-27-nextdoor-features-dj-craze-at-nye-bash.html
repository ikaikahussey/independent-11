Nextdoor in Chinatown is hosting a New Year’s Eve party, CRAZE NYE 2011, on Friday, December 31. The party features one of the world’s most celebrated club deejays: DJ CRAZE. Organizers of the party got to work early in 2010, outbidding all other clubs worldwide to get the most decorated deejay ever to Honolulu for the celebration.

Also to be featured at the event is a laser light show developed by the Drop Shadows Crew. Partyyyface will be on hand to snap photos of New Year revelers. Party goers will be able to enjoy a variety of hosted adult beverages from 9:00 p.m. to 12:00 a.m. and a complimentary champagne toast at midnight.

The three party packages available are as follows:

$20—entry and champagne toast
$50—entry, assorted hosted beverages, champagne toast
$200—VIP entry and table, bottle of Gray Goose Vodka, Red Bull, and Mixers

For more information, visit NextdoorHNL.com.

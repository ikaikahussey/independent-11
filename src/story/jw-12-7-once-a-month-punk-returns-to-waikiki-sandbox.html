Once-A-Month Punk returns to the Waikiki Sandbox on Saturday, December 18 with a roster of veteran bands.

86 List, False Crack, Dicks of Doom, Hell Caminos, and White Rose from Maui fill a bill that takes full advantage of the Sandbox’s 4:00 a.m. closing time. Stunt Dog Productions and House of Flys, who sponsor the event, will need every minute to fit in all of the punk rock.

Saturday, December 18 from 10:00 p.m. to 4:00 a.m.
Waikiki Sandbox
2260 Kuhio Ave.
Ages 21+, $5

Nextdoor in Chinatown is throwing a First Friday bash that stands out for two reasons. One, The Deadbeats are performing, and, two, they’re playing with no cover charge. The party starts at 7:00 p.m., with $3 drink specials going on until 9:00 p.m. and DJ Jami and DJ Compose will be spinning the music into the small hours.

With the 7:00 start time, Nextdoor’s event offers a fine reason to get to Chinatown early and take in some of the galleries and grubberies.

Deadbeats on First Friday
Friday, February 4
Nextdoor
43 N. Hotel
7:00 p.m.

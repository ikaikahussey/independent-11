The minutes of the July 20 Nuuanu/Punchbowl Neighborhood Board have been made available here.

HPD reported June 2010 response statistics in Districts One and Five, parts of both districts comprising the Nuuanu area. A total of three assaults, eight burglaries and six DUIs were among the responses noted. Representatives of the Board Water Supply, Honolulu Fire Department, and Acting Mayor Kirk Caldwell were on hand to give testimony, as were members and representatives of elected City and State officials.

Get ready to laugh till it hurts this weekend. The 2010 Improvaganza! Hawaii Festival of Improv begins today (Thursday, September 23) at Mark’s Garage on Nuuanu Avenue in Chinatown. This year, the festival will host well over one dozen accomplished local and national improv groups.

Laughtrack Theater will also feature several groups as part of the festival on Friday and Saturday.

Among the most notable national groups performing at Improvaganza! are Girls Girls Girls from Texas and Hello Laser from New York City.

A complete listing of shows and times is available at www.improvaganza.com.

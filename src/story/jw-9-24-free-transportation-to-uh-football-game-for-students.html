University of Hawaii Athletics and the Chancellor’s office are springing for free transportation to Aloha Stadium for Saturday’s game against Charleston Southern for UH Manoa students. The first of 10 buses will depart at 3:15 p.m. Saturday from East West Road and Dole Street. The last leaves at 3:30 p.m. All buses will leave Aloha Stadium for the UH campus 30 minutes after the game’s conclusion. Seating is available on a first-come, first-served basis. Each bus has a capacity of 48 persons.

UH Manoa students with valid identification receive free admission, as seats are available, to all UH Football and Wahine Volleyball games. 

For more information, visit http://www.hawaiiathletics.com/ .

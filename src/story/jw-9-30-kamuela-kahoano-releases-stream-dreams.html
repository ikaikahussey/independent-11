Singer-songwriter Kamuela Kahoano holds a CD release party for his new solo album of original music Stream Dreams at Pakele Live in the Ala Moana Hotel Thursday night. On hand to perform and to help Kamuela celebrate will be Manoa DNA, The Analogic, and a host of other deejays and performers.

Kamuela himself will be backed by a full band featuring Selwyn Valdez, Jason Tom, Katana, Jeff James, Mark Tanoye, and Brent Paul.

The concert will be filmed and broadcast live on www.pakelelive.com. Light pupus will be available. Seating is limited to 100 persons, so attendees are advised to arrive early.

Strem Dreams release party with Kamuela Kahoano
Thursday, September 30 at 6:00 p.m.
Pakele Live
410 Atkinson Drive

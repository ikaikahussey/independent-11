KITV4 reports:

All Kaiser Permanente clinics on Oahu, Maui and the Big Island are closed. Moanalua is open only for urgent care and emergencies.

Via @HPH_Hospitals on Twitter: “Kapiolani, Pali Momi, Straub & Wilcox are accepting emergencies. Pls keep in mind that hospitals are not emergency shelters.”

The garden is part of the ROOTS initiative of Kokua Kalihi Valley, a program which is helping valley residents to build garden boxes and grow food using organic gardening techniques. 

KKV’s Executive Director, Dr. David Derauf said, “We are excited to be a part of creating this garden which has been part of many peoples vision for this community for a long time.”

The gardening project is funded by the Centers for Disease Control and Prevention.

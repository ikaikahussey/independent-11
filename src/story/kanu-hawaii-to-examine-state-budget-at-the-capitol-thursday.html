The non-profit community activism group Kanu Hawaii will hold the “Focus on the Budget” informational briefing at the State Capitol Thursday, March 3 at 5:30 p.m. The meeting will shed light in the often opaque process of creating and operating the state budget.

Kanu Hawaii’s series “Rotunda Roundup” gathers every Thursday at the State Capitol to examine aspects of how the Legislature works and to encourage Hawaii residents to become a part of the process.

For more information, visit kanuhawaii.org.

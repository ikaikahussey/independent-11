MAUI— Trash pickups were missed today in the Kihei area by Gas Express due to staff shortage, the County of Maui’s Solid Waste Division announced today.

Streets along the affected route include Ohukai Road, Hoalike Street, Hale Kai Street, Oe Street, Noe Street, Wainohia Street, Huaka Street, Palanehe Street, Moi Place, and all surrounding streets.

Every effort will be made to pick up the refuse on Tuesday, January 25 when crews are available, County officials said.

For more information, call the Department of Environmental Management, Solid Waste Division, at (808) 270-7875.

The Kiwanis Club of Kaneohe will hold the 49th Annual Mother’s Day Pancake Breakfast at Benjamin Parker Elementary School on May 9 from 6:30 a.m. to 11:00 a.m.  For tickets contact Joshua Chang, 392-3731. This is the major fund raiser of the year and supports Kiwanis Projects in the Kaneohe community.

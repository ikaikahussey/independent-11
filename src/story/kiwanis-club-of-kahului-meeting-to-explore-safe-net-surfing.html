George Cabanting of Oxford Computer Services LLC will be the guest speaker at the Kiwanis Club of Kahului meeting on Tuesday, January 25 at noon. 

Cabanting will discuss “Safe Internet Surfing” as well as answer questions relating to the following issues:

PC and Server Support
Networks
System Administrators
Data Security
Technology Planning.

The lunch meeting is open to the public and takes place at Cary and Eddie’s Hideaway Restaurant in Kahului. Lunch is available at a cost of $11 per person.

For more information, call Bruce McDonald at (808) 283-4189.

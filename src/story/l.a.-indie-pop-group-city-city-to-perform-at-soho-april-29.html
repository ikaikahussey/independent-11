Jet Setter Productions, the folks who brought English Beat to Honolulu recently, present the Los Angeles indie electro-pop outfit CiTY CiTY to SoHo Mixed Media Bar on Friday, April 29. The band makes the stop in Honolulu as part of their spring national tour.

CiTY CiTY has enjoyed some critical acclaim for its music video “The Network,” which was a featured on industry barometer OurStage.com. The five-piece band has also garnered the favor of indie pop fans from here to Germany.

Several of Honolulu’s own indie darlings will support the bill: The Jumpoffs, GRLFRNDS, and Sing the Body.

Friday, April 29
SoHo Mixed Media Bar
80 South Pauahi Street
8:00 p.m.
$10 21 and over, $15 18-21

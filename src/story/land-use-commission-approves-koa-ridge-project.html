The State Land Use Commission on Thursday voted to recommend Castle and Cooke’s Koa Ridge housing project in Central Oahu. The project calls for 3,500 homes, schools, and commercial areas on the land between Mililani and Waipio, plus land across the H-2 Freeway from Costco. KITV4 News reports.

HONOLULU—The Land Use Commission is hearing testimony about Tropic Land LLC’s request to rezone agricultural land in Lualualei for industrial use. A hearing starts at 9:30 a.m. on Thursday, September 9 in Kapolei then continues Friday morning in Honolulu.

Kapolei hearing
Thursday, September 9 at 9:30 a.m.
Conference Room 111 A& B, Kakuhihewa Bldg.
601 Kamokila Blvd.

Honolulu hearing
Friday, September 10 at 9:00 a.m.
Conference Room 405, Fourth Floor, Leiopapa A Kamehameha,
235 S. Beretania Street

Tropic Land LLC seeks to amend the Waianae district’s Agricultural Land Use District Boundaries into the Urban Land Use District for approximately 96.0 acres in Lualualei.

For more information, visit http://luc.state.hi.us.

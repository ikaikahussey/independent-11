The Land Use Commission has announced its agenda for its Honolulu meetings on March 18 and 19. The Commission will continue its hearing on Castle & Cooke Homes Hawaii, Inc. (Oahu), which asks to amend the Agricultural Land Use District Boundary into the Urban District for about 767 acres at Waipio and Waiawa for the planned Koa Ridge development.

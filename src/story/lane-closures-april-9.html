Joseph P. Leong Highway (Haleiwa bypass road)

1)  Haleiwa
Single lane closures on the Joseph P. Leong Highway (Haleiwa Bypass Road) near the northern intersection of Kamehameha Highway and the Haleiwa Bypass Road on Monday, April 5, through Friday, April 9, from 8:30 a.m. to 3:00 p.m., daily, for traffic sensor installations. One lane will be closed in either direction and traffic will be contraflowed in remaining open lane.


Kamehameha Highway

1)  Pupupkea
Single lane closures in the southbound direction from Puula Road to Pupukea Road (near Foodland) on Monday, April 5, through Friday, April 9, from 8:30 a.m. to 3:00 p.m., daily, for manhole installations.

2)  Wahiawa to Mokuleia
Roving single lane closures in both directions from Wahiawa to Mokuleia on Monday, April 5, through Tuesday, April 6, from 8:00 a.m. to 2:00 p.m., daily, for pothole patching.

3)  Laie
Roving single lane closures in both directions from Naniloa Loop to Aakahi Gulch Road (near the Polynesian Cultural Center) on Tuesday, April 6, through Friday, April 9, from 8:30 a.m. to 2:30 p.m., daily, for traffic button installations and lane striping.

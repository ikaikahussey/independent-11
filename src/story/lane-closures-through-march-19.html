H-2 Freeway

1)  Waipahu to Wahiawa

Short duration right and left lane closures in both directions from the H-1 Freeway to the Wahiawa Off-Ramps (Exit 8) on Monday, March 15, through Friday, March 19, from 8:30 a.m. to 3:00 p.m., daily, for traffic camera sensor maintenance.

2)  Near Schofield Barracks

Single and turn lane closures in both directions at the Kaukonahua Road—Kamananui Road—Kamahehameha Highway Triangle north of Wahiawa on Monday, March 15, through Friday, March 19, from 8:30 a.m. to 3:00 p.m., daily, for pavement reconstruction.  For more information and for closure locations and hours, please contact US Army Garrison-Hawaii, Public Affairs at 656-3157 or visit http://www.garrison.hawaii.army.mil/sites/traffic/traffic.asp.

Lane closure schedules may change at any time without further notice. All projects are weather-permitting.

For island wide lane closures please visit http://hawaii.gov/dot/highways/roadwork/oahu.

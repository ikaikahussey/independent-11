Laie

Various lane closures on Kamehameha Highway in Laie between Hale Laa Boulevard and Anemoku Street fronting Laie Shopping Center on Monday, March 1, through Friday, March 5, from 8:30 a.m. to 2:30 p.m., daily, for walkway repairs.

Lane closure schedules may change at any time without further notice. All projects are weather-permitting.

For island wide lane closures please visit http://hawaii.gov/dot/highways/roadwork/oahu.

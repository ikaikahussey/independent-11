HONOLULU—Representatives of two of Hawaii’s largest civic organizations will appear on Hawaii Public Radio’s talk show “Town Square,” moderated by respected radio host and The Hawaii Independent columnist Beth-Ann Kozlovich.

The show airs live on Thursday, August 26 from 5:00 p.m. to 6:00 pm on KIPO Radio. The call-in number on Oahu is (808) 941-3689; toll free from the neighbor islands at 1-877-941-3689. AARP Hawaii State Director Barbara Kim Stanton and Hawaii League of Women Voters volunteer communications advisor Scott Foster will discuss the impact of over-50 voters on the elections this fall.

The mission of the Hawaii League of Women Voters is to educate Hawaii’s electorate about political candidates and important current issues.

AARP is a membership organization for people aged 50-and-up dedicated to serving the needs and interests of more than 147,000 members in Hawaii.

For more information, visit hawaiipublicradio.org.

KANEOHE—The 36th Annual Makahiki Fun Fair returns to Kaneohe on Saturday, July 9 at the Kaneohe District Park.

The fair features entertainment by summer fun and teen program participants, food, carnival games, pool rides, and activities from 9:00 a.m. to noon.  The family-oriented event is presented by the Kaneohe Lions Club in cooperation with the City Department of Parks and Recreation Kaneohe Complex.

Since 1975, the Makahiki Fun Fair has provided wholesome activities and enjoyment for families in the Kaneohe area.  Proceeds from the event go to support recreation programs in the Kaneohe Complex. 

For more information contact Linda Liu at (808) 233-7306.

36th Annual Makahiki Fun Fair
Saturday, July 9 from 9:00 a.m. to 12:00 p.m.
Kaneohe District Park

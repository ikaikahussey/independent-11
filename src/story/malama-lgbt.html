Native Hawaiian activists, scholars, and community leaders are inviting the community to “Malama LGBT ‘O Hawaii Maoli,” a one-day talk story and conference by and for Native Hawaiians supporting LGBT ohana.

The talk story hosted by Equality Hawaii takes place on Saturday July 3 from 9:00 a.m. to 4:00 p.m. at Halau O Haumea, 2645 Dole Street, on the University of Hawaii Campus. The talk is free and lunch is provided. Parking at UH parking garage. For more information, visit www.equalityhawaii.org. To RSVP, visit https://equalityfederation.salsalabs.com/o/35008/p/salsa/event/common/public/?event_KEY=76.

Based in Kailua, Manoa Chocolate is setting a powerful example for our local economy: they’re producing their chocolate right here in the islands.

Spearheaded by Dylan Butterbaugh, the small company is raising money to expand their operations, so that they can bring their excellent product into retail stores. They set a goal of $15,000 on Kickstarter, and as of this writing, they’re at $17,831. Check out their project here.

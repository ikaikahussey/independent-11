The public is invited to the Hamakua Community Resource Fair on March 21, from 10:00 a.m. to 1:00 p.m. at the Honokaa County Gym on Hawaii Island.

The Hamakua Resource Fair will bring together government, financial, educational, health care, and community groups in an effort to share information with the residents of Hamakua about the programs and services that are available to them.

Athletes and scholars from Honokaa for their respective achievements over the last year will also be honored.

The Fair is free and open to the public and lunch will be served.

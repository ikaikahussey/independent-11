Kailua and Kaneohe may experience increased noise from Marine Corps Base Hawaii due to an anti-terrorism exercise on Sept. 6 from 9 a.m. to 11:30 a.m. and from 1 p.m. to 4:30 p.m., according to military officials.

During this time, local and military emergency vehicles will respond to exercise incidents.

Simulated explosions will create noise and smoke throughout the morning and afternoon training sessions.

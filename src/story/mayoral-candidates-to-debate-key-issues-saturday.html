The debate is sponsored by the Hawaii Independent, the League of Women Voters of, and KTUH-FM. Professor Aviam Soifer, dean of the William S. Richard School of Law, will moderate.

Live broadcast of the debate will be provided by ‘Olelo.

Debate questions

The Hawaii Independent is actively soliciting community input on questions to pose to the candidates. To submit a question, post them as a comment on this link, or reach us via Twitter, Facebook, or email. Traditional mail is fine, too; you can reach us at:

Deadline for questions is October 3. On that date, a committee comprising Hawaii Independent editorial board members and our debate partners will meet to finalize questions.

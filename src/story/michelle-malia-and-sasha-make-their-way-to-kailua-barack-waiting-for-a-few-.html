Hawaii Magazine’s Catherine Toth blogs about the arrival of the “First Family” on Oahu for the holidays. Michelle, Malia, and Sasha will again be staying in Kailua, in the same 7,000-square-foot beachfront home on Kailuana Place the family has rented for the last three Christmases.

The President is slated to join them here in the islands once a few more things are settled on Capitol Hill, like the New START arms-control treaty and the 9/11 Health Bill.

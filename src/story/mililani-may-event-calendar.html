Town Center of Mililani’s event highlights and activities for May 2010:

(Free monthly activities)

Bungie the Clown Show
Every Monday, 10:30 am - 11:00 am
at Center Stage
Clown show, story telling and crafts

Aloha Friday Music
Every Friday, 7:00 pm - 8:00 pm
At Center Stage
Enjoy live hula or music. Please check monthly calendar for specific performance dates.

Family Bingo
Every Friday, 6:30 pm - 7:00 pm
Play two games of bingo and win 20 prizes

Chess and Checker Play
Every Saturday, 10:30 am - 12:00 pm
Near Price Busters
Learn or play the game with others

Puzzle Play
Every Saturday, 10:30 am - 12:00 pm
Near Price Busters
Enjoy new brain teasers and puzzles

Music at Sunset
Every Saturday, 7:00 pm - 8:00 pm
At Center Stage
Enjoy the music of live local bands.
Please check monthly calendar for specific performance dates.

Funtake Crafts
Make and take a free craft item under the tent next to Munchkins & Price Busters every month. Dates and times to be announced each month.

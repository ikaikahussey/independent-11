HONOLULU—On Wednesday, June 8, the Mission Houses Museum presents “Spooky Kine Investigations: Tales of Real Life Ghost Hunters” as part of its Myths, Legends and Fables performance series.

Spooky Kine Investigations provides professional paranormal investigation and consultation all over Hawaii. The organization is also committed to preserving the rich storytelling tradition that moral serves to entertain, and, more importantly teach people the moral and cultural legacies that make Hawaii so unique.

For more information, visit www.spookykine.com or www.missionhouses.org.


“Spooky Kine Investigations: Tales of Real Life Ghost Hunters”
Wednesday, June 8 at 5:30 p.m.
Mission Houses Museum
553 South King St.

WAIKIKI—On Monday, January 17, at 9:00 a.m., the 23rd Annual Martin Luther King Junior Parade will travel down Kalakaua Avenue from Magic Island, culminating in a post-parade Unity Rally at Kapiolani Park. After the parade, the bandstand comes alive with stellar music and entertainment.

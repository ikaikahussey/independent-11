At 10:23 a.m., the National Weather Service has extended a flash flood warning for the island of Oahu until 1:15 p.m. Friday. The original warning was scheduled to expire at 10:30 a.m. Follow The Hawaii Independent‘s tweets at the hashtag #oahuflashfloodwarning.

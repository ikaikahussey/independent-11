Oahu lane closures for the week of Saturday, May 8, to Friday, May 14.

1) Haleiwa
Single lane closures on the Joseph P. Leong Highway (Haleiwa Bypass Road) between Kawailoa Drive and Cane Haul Road, near the northern intersection with Kamehameha Highway, on Monday, May 10, through Friday, May 14, from 8:30 a.m. to 3:00 p.m., daily, for traffic sensor installations. One lane will be closed in either direction and traffic will be contraflowed in remaining open lane.

2) Pupukea
Single lane closures on Kamehameha Highway in the southbound direction from Puula Road to Pupukea Road, near the Foodland supermarket, on Monday, May 10, through Friday, May 14, from 8:30 a.m. to 3:00 p.m., daily, for water line installations.

3) Wahiawa to Mokuleia
Roving single lane closures on Kamehameha Highway in both directions from California Avenue in Wahiawa to Cane Haul Road in Mokuleia on Monday, May 10, through Friday, May 14, from 8:00 a.m. to 2:00 p.m., daily, for pothole patching.

Lane closure schedules may change at any time without further notice. All projects are weather-permitting.

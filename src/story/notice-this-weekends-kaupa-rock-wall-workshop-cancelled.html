A KAUPA rock wall building workshop scheduled for Saturday, December 18 and Sunday, December 19 from 9:00 a.m. to 3:00 p.m. has been cancelled due to unforseen circumstances.

KAUPA works with volunteers and the Kalihi community to restore and preserve the natural and cultural resources of Kalihi Valley through education and community service.

For more information, contact kaupa4kalihi@hawaii.rr.com.

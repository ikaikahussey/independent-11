Just a reminder that the meeting for the Koolau Loa Sustainable Communities Plan with the Public Advisory Committee (PAC) happens tonight, November 4 from 6:30 p.m. to 8:00 p.m. at the tiny Hauula Community Park Building (next to Hauula Elementary School).

The Koolau Loa PAC meeting is open to the public.

Get there early or bring a lawn chair and pupus as there will probably be standing room only.

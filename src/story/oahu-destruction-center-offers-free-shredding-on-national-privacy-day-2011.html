You can never be too cautious in today’s age of identity theft. To ensure confidential documents do not fall into the wrong hands, Aiea’s Access Information Management is offering the public free shredding of up to 5 banker boxes of confidential personal or business documents.

The free shredding happens on January 28, starting at 10:00 a.m. at Access Information Management’s Oahu Destruction Center in recognition of National Privacy Day 2011. Additional items can be destroyed for a fee. All resulting materials will be recycled.

For more information, visit www.accesscorp.com or www.dataprivacyday2011.org.

Free shredding event in recognition of National Privacy Day 2011
Friday, January 28 from 10:00 a.m. to 3:00 p.m.
Access Information Management Oahu Destruction Center
98-736 Moanalua Loop
(Across from Bed, Bath, & Beyond in Aiea)
Open to all members of the public
Secure shredding of first 5 standard size banker boxes of old records is free. Additional items will be destroyed for a fee.

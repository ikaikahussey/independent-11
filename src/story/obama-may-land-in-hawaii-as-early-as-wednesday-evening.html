CNN reported that federal officials in Hawaii are planning for President Barack Obama to arrive on Oahu as early as Wednesday, when they are instituting a no-fly zone over this city. CNN estimated that the president could technically still hold his scheduled signing ceremony of the repeal of “Don’t Ask, Don’t Tell” on Wednesday morning, hold a news conference, and still make it to Hawaii in time for dinner.

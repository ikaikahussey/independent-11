Kick off the Halloween weekend with a live hip hop-jazz-funk extravaganza featuring Hawaii’s premiere jazz quartet Quadpod. Emcees Intrepid, Joel Spiral and Seph1 will also be on the mic for a thriller night of chilling beats.

Also performing will be the Waikiki B-boys and the Rhythm Renegades featuring DJ Memoirs.

Two live sets go off at 8:30 p.m. and 11:30 p.m.

A costume contest at midnight awards a $200 first prize.

Cover is $10 at the door, $5 with costume.

Hip Hop Halloween
Friday, October 29 from 8:30 p.m.
Bambu Two, The Venue
1146 Bethel St.

Equality Hawaii Foundation is a diverse collection of individuals and organizations dedicated to securing equality for gay, lesbian, bisexual, and transgender people and their families in Hawaii.

Find out what Equality Hawaii has planned for the coming year and how to participate in their various committees. Volunteer positions are available.

For more information, email info@familyequalitycoalition.org.

Equality Hawaii Quarterly Meet-up
Saturday, October 30 from 10:30 a.m. to 12:00 p.m.
Hula’s Bar and Lei Stand
134 Kapahulu Ave., Second Floor

Jeremiah Cunningham of the Coyote Creek Farm in Elgin, Texas will be visiting Oahu on October 4 to share his knowledge with farmers about organic farming. His informative talk will include information about organic chicken and egg farming and the growing and processing of organic feed for chickens, cows, and pigs.

The event, which includes a potluck meal, will be held at North Shore’s Tin Roof Ranch across from Chun’s Reef. Luann and Gary of the Tin Roof Ranch process organic chicken and sell organic eggs. The event is free. Guests are asked to bring a healthy potluck dish.

Cunningham will also be available for individual and personalized farm consultations while he is on the island. Contact Pamela Boyar at (808) 388-9696 or haleiwafarmersmarket@gmail.com for more information.


Informative talk and potluck with Jeremiah Cunningham
Monday, October 4 from 4:00 p.m. to 9:00 p.m.
Tin Roof Ranch
61-470 Kamehameha Highway
Tin Roof Ranch tours also happening from 4:00 p.m. to 5:30 p.m.

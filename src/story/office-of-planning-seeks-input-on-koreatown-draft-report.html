Hawaii has a long and enduring relationship with Korea. In recognition of this fact, the State of Hawaii’s Office of Planning is exploring the establishment of a Koreatown in Honolulu.

A draft report, containing the results the Office of Planning’s engagement with community stakeholders, is available for download and review. The Office is looking for comments from the general public by noon on Friday, December 13, 2013.

The final report will be submitted to the Legislature later in December.

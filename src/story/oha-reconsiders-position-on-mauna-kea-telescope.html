The Office of Hawaiian Affairs is considering a resolution that would change its position on the Thirty-Meter Telescope, a massive new observatory on the summit of Mauna Kea.

The resolution is scheduled to be heard on the Board of Trustee’s Thursday agenda, which begins at 9 am.

HONOLULU—Honolulu has become a hotbed of film talent, now home to a number of notable film festivals. Rejoining that roster after a four-year hiatus is the ‘Ohina Short Film Showcase, which is open to all submission meeting festival criteria.

After a four-year hiatus, the popular Ohina Short Film Showcase is back and calling for submissions. The Hawaiian word Ohina means “the gathering” or “coming together.” Thus, this short film showcase provides a unique venue for Hawaii’s filmmakers to come together and present their creative talents to our local community. The showcase will be held at the Honolulu Academy of Arts Doris Duke Theatre at 900 S. Beretania Street on Friday, August 13 and Saturday, August 14. In addition, the showcase will be traveling to Kona for a special showing at the historic Aloha Theatre on Saturday, September 18.

Eligibility Criteria

  * Entry is open to all filmmakers regardless of age or experience.
  * Films must be made in the State of Hawaii or by filmmakers who are Hawaii residents. This includes residents that are either attending school or temporarily working out of state.
  * Films must have been completed between January 1, 2009 to June 4, 2010.
  * Films should be no more than 30 minute in length. Ohina encourages film shorts to have running times between 10 to 15 minutes.
  * Films should be submitted on DVD.
  * Films should be completed.  Rough-cuts or works-in-progress will not be accepted.

Submission Information

  * $20.00 submission fee for each film.
  * Entry submission form is available at www.ohina.org
  * Films need to be submitted no later than 5:00 p.m., Friday, June 4, 2010.
  * Include a stamped self addressed envelope, if you wish to have the film returned.

Please send submissions with signed forms to: Ohina Short Film Showcase, c/o 1013 Kawaiahao Street, Honolulu, HI 96814.

Ohina Short Film Showcase is produced by a dedicated group of volunteers in the arts and film industry. Ohina’s board of directors appoints an independent judging panel who decides on the short list to be screened during the showcase over two days. All selected filmmakers will be e-mailed confirmations and invited to the 2010 showcase.

Questions please e-mail info@ohina.org.  For additional information, visit www.ohina.org.

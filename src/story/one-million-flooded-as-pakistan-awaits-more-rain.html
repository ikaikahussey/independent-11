Fears are growing in Pakistan that floods which have hit one million people could spread out of the country’s ravaged north-west. Some 800 people are believed to have died in the worst monsoon floods in living memory. Officials say 19,000 people in the worst-hit areas have been rescued by 30,000 troops. BBC reports.

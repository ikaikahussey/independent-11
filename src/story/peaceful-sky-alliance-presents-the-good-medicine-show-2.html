The Peaceful Sky Alliance, 10,000 Gardens Project, and Friends for Justice presents The Good Medicine Show 2 with Clan Dyken, Kaliko Guys, and The Iris Eve Band—a show featuring tribal beats, electric rock, and multimedia.

* Friday, March 5 at the S.P.A.C.E. in Seaview Estates, Puna
* Saturday, March 6 at the Palace Theater, Hilo

All shows start at 7:00 p.m. Doors open at 6:00 p.m. $15 presale and $20 at the door.

Donate a non perishable food item and get $2 off the door price.

For more information, visit http://peacefulskyalliance.com.

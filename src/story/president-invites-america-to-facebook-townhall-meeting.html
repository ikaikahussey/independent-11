President Barrack Obama invites you to participate in his “Shared Responsibility and Shared Prosperity” Facebook town hall event on Wednesday, April 20 at 1:45 p.m. PT/4:45 p/m/ ET live online from Facebook headquarters in Palo Alto, California. The event will be live streamed at facebook.com/WhiteHouse or http://apps.facebook.com/facebooklive and at WhiteHouse.gov/live.

HONOLULU—Since August 29, Olelo Community Media has been providing Hawaii viewers an extensive portfolio of Primary Elections programming, including debates, forums, and candidate profiles. Up until the Primary Elections on Saturday, September 18, election shows will air daily from 7:00 a.m. to 10:00 a.m. and 7:00 p.m. to 10:00 p.m.

Election information can also be found on Olelo’s video-on-demand web service at www.olelo.org/vote.

Last month, Olelo achieved a new milestone with the submittal of the 100,000th community program. The show, Buddah Sutra was submittd by producer Em Ngoc Trinh.

For more information, visit olelo.org.

Pacific Scoop reports on a defamation case involving allegations on a popular talkback radio about the Samoan justice minister that has divided local media opinion about the issue. Alofa Tautai, 35, was fined WS$10,000 by the Supreme Court for defaming the Minister of Justice, Unasa Mesi Galo, in an a radio comment made in October 2009.

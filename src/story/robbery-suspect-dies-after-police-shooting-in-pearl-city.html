Just before 3:30 p.m. Tuesday afternoon, police shot and killed a man suspected of robbing Pukana La Massage Therapy, an upstairs businesses at Waimalu Shopping Center. This is the second deadly police shooting on Oahu this month. On April 14, police shot and killed 28-year-old Mark Ahnee after he and another man were involved in a carjacking rampage through east Honolulu. KHON2 News reports.

EWA—A town hall meeting has been scheduled for Wednesday, September 1 from 7:00 p.m. to 8:30 p.m. at Asing Park, 91-1450 Renton Road.

This meeting will be an opportunity for the community to share its concerns to elected officials in lieu of the cancelled Ewa Neighborhood Board meetings for August and September.

Agenda
1. Introduction by Representative Kymberly Pine
2. Department of Transportation Director Brennon Morioka
3. Public forum to share community issues and concerns
4. Adjournment

For more information, contact Rep. Kymberly Pine at (808) 586-9730 and pine@capitol.hawaii.gov.

Pride At Work Hawaii is holding a pau hana paina and talk story Friday, September 3 at 5:30 p.m. at Cafe Hula Girl. The talk story’s focus will be on the upcoming primary elections and the results of Pride At Work Hawaii’s first ever survey of candidates for governor, lieutenant governor, State Senate, and State House of Representatives.

Expect great discussion and free pupus. All who support economic and social justice are welcome.

Pride At Work Hawaii advocates for full equality for LGBITQ workers in their workplaces and their unions, and works to build mutual support between the labor movement and the LGBITQ community. Pride At Work Hawaii (an affiliate of the Hawaii State AFL-CIO) is a chapter of Pride At Work, a constituency group of the AFL-CIO.

For more information, visit http://www.hawaflcio.org/PAWHI.

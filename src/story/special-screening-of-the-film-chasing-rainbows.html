Citizens For Equal Rights (CFER) is holding a special screening of the film, “Chasing Rainbows,” tonight, May 14, at 6:45 p.m., at Calvary by the Sea Lutheran Church, 5339 Kalanianaole Highway. 

This movie is being shown to help educate the public on the issue of civil unions in the state of Hawaii. To learn more about the film and to watch the trailer visit: www.chasingrainbowsfilm.com.

For more information on CFER, go to www.equality808.com or call 528-6888.

The State Senate has rejected Gov. Linda Lingle’s nomination of Katherine Leonard to be Chief Justice of the Hawaii Supreme Court. The Senate voted 14-8 against Leonard on Friday, shooting down the Republican governor’s choice to lead the State Judiciary. KHON2 News has the story and a breakdown of the votes.

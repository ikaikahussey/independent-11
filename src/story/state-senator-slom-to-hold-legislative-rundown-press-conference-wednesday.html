HONOLULU — State Senate Minority Leader Sam Slom will hold a press conference on Wednesday, May 4, to discuss a variety of issues and legislative news from what a press release calls the “freedom and liberty perspective.”

Topics to be discussed include, but are not limited to the following:

A Review of the 2011 Legislative Session
The State Budget
Funding Vehicles – New Taxes, Special Fund Raids & More
State Public Worker Unions
Hawaii Education
Hawaii Council of Revenues Projections – Next Meeting

Wednesday, May 4
Hawaii State Capitol
Room 214
10:00 a.m.

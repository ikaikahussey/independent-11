What:  Sunset Beach Elementary School P.T.C.A. Family Fun Fair

When: Saturday, March 13, 10:00 a.m. - 3:00 p.m.

Where:Sunset Beach Neighborhood Park

Join us at our annual Family Fun Fair to kick off your Spring Break!

Game booths!  Prizes!  Food & Drinks!

Rides provided by Xtreme Fun Rentals such as Turbo Tubs, Xtreme Slide, Rat Race Obstacle Course, Candy Land Play Zone, Xtreme Jumper and a Rock Wall climb!

Purchase your Xtreme Fun wristband for UNLIMITED entrance on ALL Xtreme Fun Rides (good for one-time Rock Wall climb). Wristbands can be purchased at the school’s office: $18 presale; $20 on day of event.

Fun Fair sponsored by the North Shore Chamber of Commerce & the Sunset Beach Elementary School P.T.C.A.

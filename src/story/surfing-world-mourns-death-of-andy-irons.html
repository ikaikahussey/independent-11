The unexpected and mysterious death of surfing champion Andy Irons has left the tight-knit surfing world saddened and stunned. From the waters of Puerto Rico to his home state of Hawaii, tributes poured out for the three-time world champion and soon-to-be father, who was found dead in his hotel bed on Tuesday in the Dallas area.

The musicians of the Honolulu Symphony offer this free concert in appreciation of the tremendous support they have received from the community. This majestic New Year’s concert continues the annual tradition and features members of the Honolulu Symphony Orchestra and the Oahu Choral Society, led by Maestra JoAnn Falletta, performing one of music’s most popular and powerful masterpieces.  The program will also include selections from Prokofiev’s ballet Romeo and Juliet.

Donations are encouraged and will benefit the musicians.  For more information visit:
www.honolulusymphonymusicians.org

Featuring JoAnn Falletta, conductor
with the Oahu Choral Society
Esther Yoo, Director
and soloists
Jacqueline Horten, Soprano
Mary Chesnut Hicks, Mezzo
Jim Price, Tenor
Valerian Ruminski, Bass

4:00pm Sunday, Dec. 27 - Blaisdell Concert Hall

Free General Admission

Tickets are available at
Blaisdell Box Office on Ward Avenue
(and at the door if they are still available)

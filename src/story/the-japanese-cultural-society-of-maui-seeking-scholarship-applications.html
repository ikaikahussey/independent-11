The Japanese Cultural Society of Maui (JCSM) is accepting applications for its annual scholarship to be awarded to a graduating high school senior who promotes and perpetuates the Japanese culture through education and/or the arts.

The Japanese Cultural Society of Maui Scholarship is an academic scholarship awarded to a graduating high school senior who is pursuing further education with the desire to promote and perpetuate the Japanese culture.

Those interested in applying for the scholarship may contact Tiffany Iida at tiida@hawaiianisp.com or (808) 276-5444, or visit www.jcsmaui.com. The scholarship application deadline is March 15.

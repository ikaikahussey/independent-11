Michael Lujan Bevacqua at minagahet.blogspot.com blogs about the U.S. military’s buildup in Guam:

“The buildup is more of a problem than a solution, not only because of things such as overpopulation, overextension of utilities, damage to economy/society, and so on, but because of the way it keeps intact (and invigorates) so many forms of dependency and inferiority that pervade this island, and finally because of the fact that so few people seem capable of considering in anything close to an objective, practical or even useful sense.”

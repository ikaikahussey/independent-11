On Thursday, July 1, TheBus fare charges will be increasing by 25 cents.

A one-way, adult cash fare will change from $2.25 to $2.50. A one-way, youth cash fare will change from $1.00 to $1.25.

Bus passes are seeing increases as well. The Adult bus pass increased from $50.00 to $60.00 monthly, and from $550.00 to $660.00 annually. The Youth bus pass increased from $25.00 to $30.00 monthly, and from $275.00 to $330.00 annually.

Monthly passes are available at Foodland Stores on Oahu, Times Supermarkets, 7-Eleven Stores, Satellite City Halls, UH Manoa Campus Center, Hawaii Pacific University Book Store, and Chit Chat Store at Bishop Square.

Annual passes are available at the Bus Pass Office, 811 Middle St., Kalihi Transit Facility.

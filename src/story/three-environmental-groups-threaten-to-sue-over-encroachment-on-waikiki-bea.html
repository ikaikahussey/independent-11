Three environmental groups are threatening to go to court over Kyoya Tower’s plan to redevelop a resort on Waikiki beach. Kyoya is asking to be exempt from rules that require beachfront buildings to be at least 100 feet from the shoreline and lower in height the closer they are to the water. KITV4 News reports.

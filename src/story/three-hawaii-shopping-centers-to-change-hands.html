Leasing and management duties at three Hawaii shopping centers are being transferred from General Growth Properties to Jones Lang LaSalle as a result of a new strategic alliance, Pacific Business News reports. The change will affect the Kings’ Shops in Waikoloa on the Big Island, Queen Kaahumanu Center in Kahului on Maui, and Windward Mall in Kaneohe on Oahu, according to General Growth Properties.

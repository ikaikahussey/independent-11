Town Center of Mililani, 95-1249 Meheula Pkwy., is located in the heart of Mililani and is home to a growing number of tenants including Times Supermarket, Longs Drugs, Consolidated Theaters, Wal-Mart, health care and professional offices, Zippys, and a variety of specialty stores and retail businesses. The shopping complex is designed to grow with the community and, when complete, the 568,000 square foot regional shopping center will be the fourth largest of its kind in the state. Here’s what’s going on this month.

Bungie the Clown show every Monday, 10:30 a.m. to 11:00 a.m. at Center Stage
Clown show, story telling, and crafts.

Aloha Friday Music every Friday, 7:00 p.m. to 8:00 p.m. at Center Stage
Enjoy live hula or music. Please check monthly calendar for specific performance dates.

Family Bingo every Friday, 6:30 p.m. to 7:00 p.m.
Play two games of bingo and win 20 prizes.

Chess and Checker Play every Saturday, 10:30 am to 12:00 p.m. near Price Busters
Learn or play the game with others.

Puzzle Play every Saturday, 10:30 a.m. to 12:00 p.m. near Price Busters
Enjoy new brain teasers and puzzles

Music at Sunset every Saturday, 7:00 p.m. to 8:00 p.m. at Center Stage
Enjoy the music of live local bands. Please check monthly calendar for specific performance dates.

Funtake Crafts
Make and take a free craft item under the tent next to Munchkins & Price Busters every month. Dates and times to be announced each month.

Asian Lion Dance Exercise Class every Saturday, 9:00 a.m. to 10:00 a.m.
Learn the art of Chinese lion dancing & exercise; at center stage

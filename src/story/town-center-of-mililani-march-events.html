Town Center of Mililani Event Highlights and Activities for March 2010

Inside/Outside Sidewalk Sale
Friday-Sunday, March 12-14, 2010
Bargains and specials at the inside/outside sidewalk sale. Sales will be happening throughout the mall and at participating Town Center merchants.

Free Monthly Activities

Bungie the Clown Show
Every Monday, 10:30 a.m. to 11 a.m. at Center Stage
Clown show, story telling and crafts

Aloha Friday Music
Every Friday, 7 p.m. to 8 p.m. at Center Stage
Enjoy live hula or music.

Family Bingo
Every Friday, 6:30 p.m. to 7 p.m.
Play two games of bingo and win 20 prizes

Chess and Checker Play
Every Saturday, 10:30 a.m. to 12 p.m. near Price Busters
Learn or play the game with others

Puzzle Play
Every Saturday, 10:30 a.m. to 12 p.m.
Enjoy new brain teasers and puzzles

Music at Sunset
Every Saturday, 7 p.m. to 8 p.m. at Center Stage
Enjoy the music of live local bands

Funtake Crafts
Dates and times announced each month. Make and take a free craft item next to Price Busters and Munchkins every month.

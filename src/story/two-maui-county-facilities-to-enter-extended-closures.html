WAILUKU, MAUI — The County of Maui Department of Parks and Recreation has announced the closure of two parks facilities this summer.

The first is the closure of the War Memorial Football Stadium from May 23 to August 25, 2011. During this time workers will be replacing lights, the PA system and all of the wood bleachers with aluminum seats. They will also be repairing track drainage, the scoreboard and the concrete.

The second closure will be of the Baldwin Beach Park Pavilion area from June 6 to September 9, 2011. During this time workers will be repairing the pavilionâ€™s wood and concrete and also replacing the roof.

While work is going on the public will not be allowed to use either facility for their own safety.

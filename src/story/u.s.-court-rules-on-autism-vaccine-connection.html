The Daily Beast’s Adam Winkler blogs about a recent ruling by a U.S. court that vaccines don’t cause autism. He asks if the ruling will put an end to parents’ fear of inoculation or only make them more skeptical:

“One of the remarkable aspects of this story is how much ordinary people distrust the major medical associations and health organizations whose sole objective is to watch out for those people’s health.”

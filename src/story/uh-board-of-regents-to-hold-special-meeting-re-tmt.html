The University of Hawaii Board of Regents will hold a special board meeting to discuss the future of the Thirty Meter Telescope (TMT) project this Thursday, April 16, at 11:30 a.m. at the University of Hawaii Hilo campus.

Written testimony on the matter will be accepted up to 24-hours ahead of the meeting and can be emailed to bor@hawaii.edu. Late testimony will be made available to the regents within 24 hours of receipt. Individuals who wish to testify in person will be allowed to during the public comment period of the meeting before the regents go into discussion. The regents are asking that testifiers limit their testimony to three minutes.

The University of Hawaii at Manoa Outreach College’s Pacific New Media presents a free video and slideshow presentation and book signing of Clark Little’s The Shorebreak Art of Clark Little on Friday, August 13.

Ocean lovers, as well as photographers and art aficionados, will enjoy breathtaking bursts of Hawaiian shorebreak surf on the big screen. The 30-minute presentation includes slideshow segments of North Shore waves set to music, in-water video footage of Clark capturing his images, older footage of Clark surfing Waimea Bay shorebreak, and insight from Clark on how he captures his stunning images. Q&A and book signing follows the presentation.

Waimea Bay shorebreak surfing pioneer, husband, and father of two, Clark Little has gained nationwide recognition for his North Shore wave photography capturing the beauty and power of monstrous Hawaiian waves from the inside out.

Over the last year, Clark has appeared on Good Morning America, Today Show, Inside Edition, and ABC World News Now. His images have also been published in Nature`s Best Photography, Paris Match, La Vie, Hana Hou (Hawaiian Airlines magazine), Surfer Magazine, Surfer`s Journal, as well as multiple newspapers in the U.S. and overseas.

Clark`s first coffee table art book, The Shorebreak Art of Clark Little, was released November 2009. 

For more information, call 956-8244 or visit www.outreach.hawaii.edu/pnm

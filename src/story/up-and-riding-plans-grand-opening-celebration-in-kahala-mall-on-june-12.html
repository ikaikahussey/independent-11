Children’s surf and skate apparel retailer Up & Riding opens this Friday in Kahala Mall.  On Saturday June 12, between 1:00 and 2:00 p.m., they are holding a special Up & Riding and Volcom event for Honolulu grommets.  Volcom team riders Bruce Irons, Dave Wassel, Gavin Beschen, and Tom Dosland will be here at the store signing autographs.  Custom screened Volcom T-shirts signed by Bruce Irons are on offer for the first 100 kids—a collectors item for any self-respecting grom.  Free posters, hats, and lots of other goodies will be given away as well.  This fun event is free and open to the public.

For more information, please visit http://www.upandriding.com/.

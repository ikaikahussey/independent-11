State Rep. Gene Ward (R) sits down with folks from Kamiloiki Elementary as part of his A Word with Ward program on Olelo. Principal Susan Okano and Curriculum/Technology Coordinator Terrie Kamo talk about efforts in meeting No Child Left Behind goals. And in an effort that’s not seen as often as it might be needed, Ward gets the perspective of a student. Fifth grade student Kalei Young is also part of the interview panel.

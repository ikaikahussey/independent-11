State Rep. Kimberly Pine (R) commented in a YouTube video on the Senate’s decision to reject Judge Katherine Leonard’s nomination for Chief Justice of the State Supreme Court. Pine said that the Senate failed to provide any legitimate reasons for its decision other than to say that “Leonard was not qualified.” Pine represents Ewa Beach, Iroquois Point, and Puuloa.

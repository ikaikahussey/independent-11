The Minority House and the Minority Senate leadership, both from East Oahu, get together in one room to talk about the 2011 session. Sole Republican Sen. Sam Slom is a guest on House Minority Leader Rep. Gene Ward’s program on Olelo titled, “A Word with Ward.”

Slom talks about what he calculates to be a $1 billion total deficit facing the State, protecting small businesses, and creating trust with Hawaii voters.

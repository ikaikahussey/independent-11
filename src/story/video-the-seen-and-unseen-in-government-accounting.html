The following video shares dialogue from an event titled “Government Accounting: The Seen and the Unseen—What Needs to be Done?” held at the Hawaii State Capitol in October. The keynote speaker was Sheila Weinberg of The Institute for Truth in Accounting.

The event was convened by Rep. Gene Ward (R - District 17) and Rep. Barbara Marumoto (R - District 19).

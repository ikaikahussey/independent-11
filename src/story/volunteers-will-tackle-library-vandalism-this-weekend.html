Mahalo Hawaii Independent for reporting on this story.  Volunteers will be tackling the cleaning job this Saturday (2/26) and Sunday (2/27) from noon-4pm.  Vaccums would be welcome, but if you even have an hour or two to spare, many hands will make the work go more quickly!  Please feel free to call 393-0594 if you have any questions about clean-up efforts.

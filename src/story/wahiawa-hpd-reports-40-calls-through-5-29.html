The Honolulu Police Department reports the calls they receive weekly regarding auto theft recovery, motor vehicle theft, unauthorized entry into a motor vehicle, burglary, theft, and graffiti type incidents for each district on the island.

For the week of 5/16/2010 to 5/29/2010, the Honolulu Police Department reported 40 calls for service for these incidents for the Wahiawa and Schofield.

For more information visit http://www.honolulupd.org/statistics/index.htm.

Wahiawa and surrounding area lane closures 5/8-5/14

1) Wahiawa to Mokuleia
Roving single lane closures on Kamehameha Highway in both directions from California Avenue in Wahiawa to Cane Haul Road in Mokuleia on Monday, May 10, through Friday, May 14, from 8:00 a.m. to 2:00 p.m., daily, for pothole patching.

2) Kunia to Schofield Barracks
Single lane closures on Kunia Road in both directions from the H-1 Freeway Overpass to Wilikina Drive on Monday, May 10, through Friday, May 14, from 8:30 a.m. to 3:00 p.m., daily, for pavement marking and traffic sensor installations.

Single lane closures on Kunia Road in both directions between Hauula Street, near Hawaii Country Club, and Plantation Road on Monday, May 10, through Friday, May 14, from 8:30 a.m. to 3:00 p.m., daily, for installation of traffic sensors.

Lane closure schedules may change at any time without further notice. All projects are weather-permitting.

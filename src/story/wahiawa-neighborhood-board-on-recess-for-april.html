The Wahiawa Neighborhood Board will be on recess for the month of April, 2010.

Committee of the Whole meeting will take place on May 10, 2010 at 6:30 p.m. at the Wahiawa Recreation Center.

Next regular board meeting will take place on Monday, May 17,  2010 at 7:00 p.m. at the Wahiawa Recreation Center

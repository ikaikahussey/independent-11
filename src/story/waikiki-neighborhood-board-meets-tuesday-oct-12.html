WAIKIKI—The Waikiki Neighborhood Board will be meeting Tuesday, October 12 at the Waikiki Community Center, 310 Paoakalani Avenue, from 7:00 p.m. until 9:30 p.m.

The meeting will feature two-term Democrat Representative Tom Brower and long time Waikiki resident and Republican Tom White, who are competing for Waikiki’s spot in the Hawaii House of Representatives. There will also be a discussion between Stanley Chang and Richard Turbin, who are vying for the City Council seat formerly held by Charles Djou (and currently by Lee Donohue) in a non-partisan race. The moderator for this event is KHON2 news anchor Olena Heu.

Following the formal program, all candidates are invited to meet with members of the audience for one-on-one discussions.

For the complete agenda, visit http://www.honolulu.nco/nb9/10/09201010Ag.PDF.

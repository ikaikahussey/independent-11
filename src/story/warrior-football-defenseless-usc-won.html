In yesterday’s Hawaii vs. USC football game, a key play just before halftime may have been the difference. Hawaii’s Greg Salas rolls into the end zone for what looks to be a touchdown just before halftime, but after appearing to signal a score, the referees decide to spot the ball just short of the goal. The Warriors settled for a field goal and trailed, 27-16, at the half. Defenseless USC won, 49-36, despite being outgained, 588-524, according to TheWizofOdds.com.

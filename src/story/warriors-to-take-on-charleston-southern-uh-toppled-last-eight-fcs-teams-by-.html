The University of Hawaii’s Warrior football team takes on Charleston Southern, a Football Championship Subdivision school, Saturday night. Hawaii has defeated its last eight FCS opponents by an average of 30 points. The Warriors’ last loss to an FCS team was the 2000 season-opening defeat by Portland State. Associated Press reports.

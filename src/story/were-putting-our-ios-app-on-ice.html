Aloha kakou:

We’re putting our iOS app on ice. Several factors influenced this decision: the Newsstand platform, upon which the app is built, is less useful under iOS 7; posting to the app increases the burden on staff; the app isn’t available for non-iOS users; and our website is already a pretty good platform for reading.

Popular recurring app content, such as Polyfantastica, will be migrated over to a new product, due out soon.

If you have any concerns or questions, please email me. We’re also happy to issue refunds for the past couple of issues which didn’t make it out while we contemplated this decision. Please get in touch.

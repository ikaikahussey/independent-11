The West Oahu Family Festival at Kapolei Regional Park happens today through Sunday. Festivities include music, food, and family entertainment.

The festival is one of several City-sponsored events designed to bring families, local vendors, and entertainers together for fun in the communities around the island of Oahu.

West Oahu Family Festival details for Friday, Saturday, and Sunday:

·    Friday, July 9 from 6:00 p.m. to midnight. Entertainment through the weekend will include Simplicity, Touch of Gold, Fab Four, Manuia, HI-Town Players, Princess Ilona Irvine, BET, and the Royal Hawaiian Band.

·    Activities continue Saturday, July 10 from noon to midnight and Sunday, July 11 from noon to 10:00 p.m. with a fashion show by Look Hawaii Magazine, Keiki rides, local food booths, and a marketplace with local craftsman, artists, a farmers market, and a health and resource tent.

On April 8, The Hawaii Publishers Association announced this year’s winners at the 25th Annual Pai Awards at the Hilton Hawaiian Village.

The Hawaii Publishers Association is a nonprofit media organization that promotes magazines, newspapers, books, web and other aspects of the publishing industry in Hawaii. The Pa’i Awards honors outstanding achievements in Hawaii publications.  An independent panel of mainland publishing experts judges the contest and has announced the awards for excellence in editorial content and graphics in numerous categories for newspapers, magazines and other publications.

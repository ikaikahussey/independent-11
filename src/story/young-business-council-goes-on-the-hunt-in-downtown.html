This afternoon, you might see members of the Young Business Council scrambling through the streets of Downtown looking for the next item on their scavenger hunt list. Or you might be scrambling around with them.

The Young Business Council Scavenger Hunt and Networking Event takes place Wednesday, June 24. And it’s open to everyone. Registration begins at 5:30 p.m. at Murphy’s Bar and Grill. Put your game faces on, there are prizes for the top teams.

The Young Business Council was founded in September 2003 with the purpose of providing a networking and professional development organization for young professionals working in all industries. The council aims to prepare young professionals by enhancing their development of project management, communication, and leadership skills.

WHAT:  YBC Scavenger Hunt and Networking Event
WHEN:  Wednesday, June 24, 5:30 p.m.
WHERE:  Murphy’s Bar and Grill, 2 Merchant Street
COST:  $25 for YBC members; $30 for non-members
Heavy pupu served, plus prizes for top teams

For more information, visit http://youngbusinesscouncil.com/.
